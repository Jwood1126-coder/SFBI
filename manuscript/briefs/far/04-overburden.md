# Nº 04 — Overburden · FAR

3rd limited, past tense, single POV — Mara Vinstrom, the station's driller, the crew of eight around her. Form: chapters headed by a borehole diagram and the refreeze depth, meters closing from above; the countdown runs upward, toward a surface they can't reach. ~105,000 words. Imagery: deep-ice drilling as practiced — hot-water thermal drilling, borehole cryostat, upward-looking sonar, ice crystallography, closed-loop life support, ascent tether logistics, drill rate against refreeze rate. Canon echo: none — the book's law is that the will above them is unreadable; any known "somebody" would hand the crew a frame it must deny them. Ending directive: the thermal drill re-vectored, the depth gauge holding a number, a gloved hand logging the reading — no gloss on the ice. Forbidden: the ocean explained, its crystal decoded into a message; a creature shown; a crewmember who communes and turns prophet. Levers: (a) rotating ensemble POV, one crew station per chapter; (b) commit the middle to the protection reading — the ascent becomes possible contamination; (c) compress to the ascent race, dropping Act I routine.
