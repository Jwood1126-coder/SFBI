# Nº 80 — Evidential · ACREAGE

3rd limited, Sana Merchant, past tense. Form: recording sessions dated by the thaw across three winters; each carries her glosses, the Vurë evidential suffixes tagged, her field-note marks failing as her source of knowledge shifts. ~4,500 words. Imagery: language documentation — recorder, ELAN annotation, morpheme glossing, elicitation over cold tea, the fine-tune corpus; fog above the Enguri gorge. Canon echo: none — the fog-locked village sits outside the world's news; its sealed remoteness is the point. Ending directive: the model's first unprompted Vurë sentence after Reveka's death — Sana naming aloud the reportative suffix it fails to carry. Forbidden: the model given pathos or a plea (it produces cold text, no sentience theater); Reveka as mystical last-elder (no folk-wisdom montage); evidentiality lectured to the reader. Levers: (a) a heritage-ministry official demos the model at a festival — the taxidermy question made public; (b) build it from the annotated transcripts, Sana's margin notes the only narration; (c) the model asserts the witnessed evidential it cannot own — a fluent lie in the grammar's forbidden form.
