# Nº 48 — Caretaker Star · FAR (Tier B)

1. **Secretly about** — Grace was never given; it was maintained — a species learns its good fortune was somebody's unthanked labor, and must decide whether to take up the same shift: a duty longer than any life, owed to no one who will ever say thank you.

2. **Sibling rule** — Nº 02 *The Custodian*. Both are inherit-the-machine-you-didn't-build books whose last beat is the job handed to you. Enforce the split: *The Custodian* mourns known dead through the manuals they left — intimate, human, one habitat; *Caretaker Star*'s maker is a billion years gone, inhuman, and left no words. The register is stellar first-contact and stewardship vertigo, not eulogy. If the caretaker becomes legible, or the drafts turn to reading a predecessor's instructions, this is only *The Custodian* scaled to a star.

3. **The two questions**
   - Outward — How do a few solar physicists confirm the impossible against every instinct to be wrong, mount a repair on machinery a million years their senior with probes that cannot survive the medium, and harden a planet's grid before the star's true weather returns?
   - Inward — What must a scientist become to accept a stewardship she will die decades before completing — to hand an unfinished, unthankable job to someone younger and call that success?

4. **Dyad**
   - Equation — the governors' damping efficiency is a decaying exponential, e-folding in decades; three clocks that will not align — returning super-Carrington flare weather, the ~22-year Hale cycle across which any repair can only be confirmed, and the multi-year lead time to build and place transformers and probes. You must commit before you can know you succeeded.
   - Mirror — Mei Chen and Idris Bello, the younger physicist she trains to hold the star after her. Her succession is the maker's succession at human scale; whether she can hand him the shift is whether the species can take it at all.

5. **Shape** — Dual close-third (Chen dominant early, Bello rising as she recedes — the POV itself performing the handoff), past tense. Three parts — Signature / Repair / Tenure. Chapter heads are a dated decay readout: the measured regulation index falling toward threshold, Hale cycles remaining beside it, so every head is the clock. ~120,000 words, ~34 chapters.

6. **The novum's price**
   - Flesh — the flare weather returns *during* the book: a super-Carrington event drops a regional grid, doses a polar flight or an orbital crew, kills. A body count accrues while they work.
   - Mass/money — the Sounder probes are one-way furnace-divers, each dataset bought with a probe and its mass gone forever into the star; the grid retrofit costs a planet its treasure on the unprovable word of a few.
   - Time — verification outlasts a life: Chen dies before one Hale cycle confirms the fix. She commits to a repair she will never see work, and spends her last years training the hand that will.

7. **Science memo pointers** — Helioseismology: p-modes and the long-sought g-modes; GONG ground network, SOHO/GOLF, SDO/HMI; inversion for the tachocline shear at ~0.7 R☉; time-distance and ring-diagram methods — the regulation must be posed as a residual that won't divide out. Dynamo/tachocline MHD: Babcock-Leighton flux transport, toroidal-field storage and magnetic-buoyancy instability (Spiegel & Zahn 1992), the 11/22-yr Hale cycle. Load-bearing real hook: the Sun is measurably quieter than its photometric twins (Reinhold et al. 2020) and Kepler/TESS superflare statistics (Maehara et al. 2012) predict Carrington-plus events far more often than Earth's record shows. Paleo record: cosmogenic C-14 (tree rings), Be-10/Cl-36 (ice cores), the Miyake events (774, 993 CE) as the extreme-SPE fingerprint. Space-weather engineering: geomagnetically induced currents, large-power-transformer lead times, the 1989 Hydro-Québec collapse (90 s), Carrington 1859, the 2012 near-miss. Probe limits and the honest failure mode: Parker Solar Probe (perihelion ~9.86 R☉, carbon-carbon TPS ~1370 °C) is the ceiling; the photosphere is 5772 K and nothing descends into it — spend the one implausibility here, honoring thermal, radiation, and ablation failure. Neutrino diagnostics: Borexino CNO/pp, Super-K/SNO as the only real-time core probe. Fix: 8.3-min light lag, neutrino-vs-photon transport, decades e-folding against billion-year suppression.

8. **Opening image candidate** — By hand Chen re-ran the inversion three times before she let the residual stand in the record: a damping term at the shear layer that no clean model of the Sun would put there, and that would not, however she weighted the modes, go to zero.

9. **Ending directive** — A handoff, not a verdict: a keeping physically passed from one hand to a younger one over a readout whose answer is still a decade from arriving — the taking of the shift, not proof the shift can be held. Object, act, or line; never a confirmation.

10. **Forbidden**
   - The caretaker as god or parent — no benevolent or malevolent personality, no message of love, no farmer's ledger. It stays inhuman, gone, and its intent stays the unanswered garden-or-harvest question.
   - Icarus/Phaethon romance — no probe "diving heroically into the sun" as spectacle; the descent is an instrument-and-telemetry problem with an unglamorous failure mode, not a sacrifice ballet.
   - Theology as engine — Earth's religious panic is texture, not spine; do not let the book become faith-versus-science debate.
   - The plucky repair triumph — the "million years too young" gap is real; the outcome is inheritance of an unfinished, unconfirmable job, not a win.
   - Explaining the calm — the day the novel fully says for whom and toward what, the mystery that carries it dies. Keep the ripening question open.

11. **Levers**
   - (a) Collapse to single close-third on Chen and end at her death mid-shift, the handoff seen only in her written instructions — darker, elegiac, forecloses the Bello thread.
   - (b) Commit the ripening toward harvest: the governors' failure is itself the scheduled event, the calm ending on purpose, so repair fights the maker's design — or invert it warmer: the keys were left deliberately, a graduation to be earned.
   - (c) Contract to the Sounder program alone — one insertion campaign, mission-clock chapters, no Earth-politics thread: a taut engineering novella of a single descent.
