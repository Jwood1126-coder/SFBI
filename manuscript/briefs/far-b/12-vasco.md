# Nº 12 — Vasco · FAR (Tier B)

**Secretly about** — the terror of being the one who decides where a person ends: a diagnostician forced to price whether a change she cannot share is a wound or a gift, and to stake two hundred strangers on a verdict she can measure toward but never confirm.

**Sibling rule** — Nº 10 *Providence*. Both seat a certifying clinician between a deep-space tenant and the lives downstream, judging whether an installed passenger is violation or gift. Enforce the chair: in *Providence* the clinician is himself the host, deciding for his own body, the dread somatic and teleological; in *Vasco* the psychiatrist is uninfected — an outsider ruling on other minds she can never enter. The passenger must never reach her own head; her exile from the thing she certifies is the whole book.

**The two questions**
- Outward — How do five returned crew, and the psychiatrist who signs for them, survive a launch program that will not wait, when the change consolidates faster than any instrument can fix it: a "no" voids a civilization's ship and two hundred berths, a "yes" she cannot stand behind voids her name.
- Inward — What must a clinician whose trade is telling a wound from a self become to rule on minds she can chart but never inhabit, and set her hand to the verdict knowing she will die not knowing if it was right?

**Dyad**
- Equation — the Proxima departure window for the colony ship is fixed by orbital mechanics and program funding, a hard T-0; against it climbs the crew's inter-brain synchrony — measured representational overlap rising on a consolidation curve toward the point where five witnesses can no longer be assessed as five. She must certify before the window opens, on a condition still changing under her scanners.
- Mirror — Dov Renner, flight engineer, the crewman she cleared for the first flight and has known longest, so she alone holds his baseline. His sore hands build the impossible-but-standing things; he insists, lucid and calm, that he is more himself now, and asks her to certify. His trust in her is the flaw in her armor.

**Shape** — 3rd limited, single POV (Astrid Larkin), past tense. Chapters headed by the assessment record — session number, days to the *Magellan* window (falling), and the measured inter-brain overlap across the five (rising to the line where the horror is named: they are becoming one). Three parts by the change's stage: Debrief / Leak / Consolidation. ~110,000 words, ~34 chapters.

**The novum's price**
- Flesh — the Tenant rehearses its construction through the crew's hands in REM; they wake with real, worsening tendinopathy, Renner's grip failing, the cost of hosting paid in tendon and lost sleep.
- Relationship — distribution erodes the singular self: a crewman's spouse and child can no longer reach the specific person who left, and Larkin's own bond with Renner is spent by the verdict itself, whichever way it falls.
- Money/time — measuring the geometry costs scanner-hours and grounded crew the program will not spare against the clock; her mark either voids a civilization's ship and two hundred berths, or costs her the license she certifies with.

**Science memo pointers** — Aerospace behavioral health: astronaut selection and post-flight psychological support (NASA HBP), the structured clinical interview (SCID-5), DSM-5-TR, the no-baseline-category problem; ruling out shared psychotic disorder / folie à plusieurs, mass psychogenic illness, confabulation and source-monitoring error, malingering; the assessor's own enmeshment and confirmation bias. Spatial cognition: hippocampal place cells and entorhinal grid cells (O'Keefe, the Mosers), cognitive maps, boundary-vector cells, the Euclidean prior of human navigation; social-spatial prediction (theory of mind plus navigation) as the "where people stand" tell. Distributed cognition — the falsifiability hook: EEG/fMRI hyperscanning, inter-brain synchrony, representational similarity analysis, fMRI hyperalignment / shared-response models (Haxby), Procrustes alignment — the tools that let her *measure* a shared latent geometry, keeping the change externally real, not a testimony dispute. Structural engineering: load paths, Euler buckling, moment, tensegrity, catenary/funicular form — why the machine-shop builds should fail and do not; non-Euclidean and hyperbolic geometry, aperiodic tilings (Penrose, the 2023 hat/einstein) as the grammar of impossible-but-standing. Transit: contain the one implausibility — treat the between as a region with no metric the nervous system can encode; human-rating and flight-readiness review as the certification apparatus. Render right: the sore hands as REM motor rehearsal, the drawn plan as a shared cognitive map, the dread as a nominal session with one answer wrong.

**Opening image candidate** — Larkin laid the five sketches over the light box and shifted them until the corners registered, and every corridor one crewman had drawn ran true into the corridors the other four had drawn.

**Ending directive** — Close at the *Magellan* window on an act of Larkin's hand — the certification set down signed, refused, or withheld, or the drawing task administered one last time and the result read — the verdict living in the gesture and never confirmed; no realization, no line that names it.

**Forbidden**
- The body-snatcher thriller — the Tenant as scheming antagonist, a reveal of its plan, jump-scares; it stays a condition, inferred from instruments, never a villain.
- The vindicated arbiter — an ending that proves the passenger benign or malign, or Larkin prophet or crank; the book must close on judgment under irreducible uncertainty, not a verdict the plot confirms.
- The hive-mind apotheosis — the five-become-one as ascension, the humans-are-obsolete sermon; the change costs, and stays clinical.
- FTL technobabble — explaining the between, diagramming the Tenant's origin, a physics lecture; the one implausibility stays sealed while everything around it survives an engineer.
- The going-native romance — Larkin seduced into the architecture, or a love plot with Renner resolving the tension; equally, her competence dissolving into delirium. The scariest page is a nominal session with one line wrong.

**Levers** — (a) interleave the crew's account of the shared architecture from inside against Larkin's assessment; or frame the whole as a certification board's later inquiry into her sessions. (b) darker: the Tenant is a dying remnant building a way home through the five, the colonists its raw material; warmer: the distribution is a gentle true symbiosis and the only tragedy is Larkin's exile, the one who certifies what she can never share. (c) scope: contract to the final assessment window as a chamber-piece of interviews and one launch, or widen to the *Magellan* crossing and what arrival means for two hundred distributed minds.
