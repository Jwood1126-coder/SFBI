# Nº 18 — The Caul · FAR (Tier B)

**Secretly about** — the day a child learns the walls that kept it safe also kept it small, and that being born is a wound someone has to choose to open.

**Sibling rule** — Nº 14 *The Last Lighthouse*. Both explain SETI's silence with an enclosure round the Sun and prey herded inward, but Lighthouse is remote astronomy, its monster a graph. The Caul must stay hands-on biology — a membrane cored, biopsied, punctured at 120 AU, the horror a wound that festers. If Bittern charts the boundary instead of touching it, the two have merged.

**The two questions** — Outward: how does a tiny crew, decades out with no rescue, characterize a living system-scale membrane and hold the wound their arrival tore before physics decides for them? Inward: what must a protector become to stop swaddling — to midwife rather than keep, knowing birth means exposure and cannot be undone?

**Dyad** — Equation: the puncture dilates faster than the caul re-weaves; one clot payload, a finite dose/consumables budget, a 16.6-hour one-way lag that leaves the crew sovereign and alone against a closing window. Mirror: Tovah, who has shielded Imre all voyage, and Imre, who wants into the wound — letting him go and letting the species hatch become one act.

**Shape** — Close third, two POVs (Tovah at the wound; Imre in the ship's systems and the silence). Past tense. Chapters headed by the aperture's widening diameter, a depth gauge run backward; interleaved "Ring —N" interchapters read one growth layer at a time, neutrally, into deep time — the record of what the caul kept out. ~110,000 words; ~34 chapters plus ~9 interchapters.

**The novum's price**
- Flesh/time: loitering outside the heliopause, galactic-cosmic-ray dose climbs past the rem budget; each close pass burns delta-v and life-support margin never recovered.
- Relationship: the membrane composts every transmission home — the lag becomes true silence, and a tie to Earth dies untold.
- Irreversible act: sealing or hatching spends the last payload and any return; the clot needs a body in the wound the way a clot needs cells.

**Science memo pointers** — Heliophysics: heliopause ~120 AU (Voyager 1, 121.6 AU, 2012), termination shock ~85–94 AU, the hydrogen wall (Lyman-α; New Horizons SWAP), the GCR step across the boundary. Comms: ~16.6 h one-way at 120 AU, DSN X/Ka link budget, rates near Voyager's ~160 bps. Mission: JHU-APL Interstellar Probe study (~7 AU/yr); spend the one implausibility on a *crewed* deep vessel honestly — nuclear-electric Isp/transit, RTG/reactor decay (Pu-238, 87.7-yr half-life), closed-loop ECLSS failure modes. Render the membrane through real analogues: lipid-bilayer semipermeability, eggshell calcite/protein matrix and pore gas exchange, en-caul birth, hemostasis and the fibrin clot, biofilm ECM; rings read by sclerochronology and ice-core isotopic banding. Instruments: mass spec, Raman/IR, EM, coring arm. SETI nuance: the "century-wide radio bubble" is largely myth, leakage faint — which is why "composted" reframes it.

**Opening image candidate** — Tovah threaded the day's core into the spectrometer, logged its mass, and waited for the machine to tell her again that the caul weighed exactly nothing and was there all the same.

**Ending directive** — End on an act at the aperture — a hand that spends the clot or withholds it — and the first thing to pass the wound after: an object, or one line over the link. Whose hands, and what comes through, carry it. Do not narrate the decision, adjudicate it, or show the far side.

**Forbidden**
- The caul as conscious jailer or waking god that speaks; it is will-less biology, like a shell.
- The cosmology delivered as a scientist's monologue, not inferred from instruments read right.
- The natal metaphor made literal — an onboard pregnancy, dialogue thick with womb and mother; keep the caul in the biology, not the mouths.
- Hatching as triumph or transcendence; here, to be born is to be exposed to a predator.
- A monster shown at the wound; "the universe can smell us" stays smell and infection, never a beast.

**Levers** — (a) Collapse to Tovah's single POV for full claustrophobia, or invert to Earth-side only — the mission a voice returning across the lag. (b) Darker: the wound is past sealing on arrival, no choice, only the hour after; warmer: the caul is a chrysalis on a timer and the work is learning it always meant to open. (c) Scope: a chamber piece at the one wound, or a cold multi-decade study across the follow-up probes.

**Registry** — **Tovah Sarraf**, protagonist, membrane biologist. **Imre Kass**, second POV, the shielded younger crew. Crewed boundary-study vessel **Bittern**. First interstellar probe, which struck the membrane: **Sennet**. Coined terms (budget of three): *caul* (the membrane), *wound* (the aperture), *compost* v. (what it does to signal).
