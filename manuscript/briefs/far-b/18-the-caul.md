# Nº 18 — The Caul · FAR (Tier B)

**Secretly about** — the day a protector learns the walls that kept the child safe also kept it small, and that being born is a wound someone has to choose to open.

**Sibling rule** — Nº 14 *The Last Lighthouse*. Both blame SETI's silence on an enclosure round the Sun that herds prey inward — but Lighthouse is remote astronomy, its monster a graph, no ship launched. The Caul is hands-on biology: a membrane cored, biopsied, punctured at 120 AU. Chart the boundary instead of touching it and they merge.

**The two questions** — Outward: how does a tiny crew, decades out with no rescue, characterize a living system-scale membrane and hold the wound their arrival tore before the window closes? Inward: what must a protector become to stop swaddling — to midwife rather than keep, when birth means exposure that cannot be undone?

**Dyad** — Equation: the puncture dilates faster than the caul re-weaves; one clot payload, a finite dose-and-consumables budget, a 16.6-hour one-way lag leaving the crew sovereign and alone against a closing aperture. Mirror: Tovah has shielded Aurel all voyage; Aurel wants into the wound — so letting him go and letting the species hatch become one act.

**Shape** — Close third, two POVs: Tovah at the wound, Aurel in the ship's systems and the silence. Past tense. Chapters head with the aperture's widening diameter — a depth gauge run backward; interleaved "Ring −N" interchapters read one ring at a time into deep time: the record of what the caul kept out. ~110,000 words; ~34 chapters, ~9 interchapters.

**The novum's price**
- Flesh/time: loitering past the heliopause, the cosmic-ray dose climbs beyond the rem budget; each close pass burns delta-v and life-support margin never recovered.
- Relationship: the membrane composts every transmission home; the lag becomes true silence, and a tie to Earth dies untold.
- Money/act: sealing or hatching spends the last clot payload and any return — the clot needs a body in the wound the way a clot needs cells.

**Science memo pointers** — Heliophysics: heliopause ~120 AU (Voyager 1, 121.6 AU, 2012), termination shock ~85–94 AU, the hydrogen wall (Lyman-α; New Horizons SWAP/Alice), the cosmic-ray step across the boundary. Comms: ~16.6 h one-way at 120 AU, DSN X/Ka, ~160 bps. Vessel (cf. JHU-APL Interstellar Probe, ~7 AU/yr): nuclear-electric Isp/transit, RTG decay (Pu-238, 87.7-yr half-life), closed-loop ECLSS failure modes. Membrane analogues: lipid-bilayer semipermeability, eggshell calcite/protein matrix and pore gas exchange, en-caul birth, hemostasis/fibrin clot, biofilm ECM; rings via sclerochronology and ice-core isotopic banding. Instruments: mass spec, Raman/IR, electron microscopy, coring arm. The century-wide radio bubble is largely myth — faint leakage, hence "composted."

**Opening image candidate** — Tovah threaded the day's core into the spectrometer, logged its mass, and waited for the machine to tell her again that the caul weighed exactly nothing and was there all the same.

**Ending directive** — End on an act at the aperture — a hand that spends the clot or withholds it — and the first thing to cross the wound after: an object, or one line over the link. Whose hand and what comes through carry it; do not narrate the choice, adjudicate it, or show the far side.

**Forbidden**
- The caul as conscious jailer or waking god that speaks; it is will-less biology, like a shell.
- The natal metaphor made literal — onboard pregnancy, mouths thick with womb and mother; keep it in the biology, not the dialogue.
- Hatching as triumph or transcendence; here, to be born is to be exposed to a predator.
- A monster shown at the wound; "the universe can smell us" stays smell and infection, never a beast.

**Levers** — (a) Collapse to Tovah's single POV, or invert to Earth-side only — the mission a voice returning across the lag. (b) Darker: the wound is past sealing on arrival, only the hour after; warmer: the caul is a chrysalis on a timer, always meant to open. (c) Scope: a chamber piece at the one wound, or a cold multi-decade study across the follow-up probes.
