# Nº 22 — Drawdown · FAR (Tier B)

**Secretly about** — occupancy: the discovery that we are not the planet's owners but its tenants, breathing air a prior life built and a prior life has come to repossess — and the vertigo of choosing whether to exterminate the landlord to keep breathing.

**Sibling rule** — Nº 03 *The Greenhouse Variable*. Both are forensic novels where a scientist reads a planetary signal and finds Earth being handed to a non-human owner, resurrection and eviction at once. Enforce the single split: Greenhouse's claimant is *authored* — a deliberate design with a maker inbound from outside; Drawdown's claimant is Earth's own *unauthored* deep past, no maker, no message, no arrival, the dispossessed native already here. The moment the sink resolves to a designer, an intention, or anything coming from beyond, Drawdown has become Greenhouse.

**The two questions**
- Outward — How do eight billion oxygen-breathers keep the grids and the lungs running while an unreachable metabolism in the crust draws the air down a tenth of a percent a year toward a floor no one can raise — and can the sink be located, let alone fought, before the number reaches the blood?
- Inward — What must Noemi, and the species behind her, become — trespasser, defender, or exterminator — to decide whether to wage planetary war for an atmosphere that was itself the loot of the first oxygen conquest?

**Dyad**
- Equation — the O₂ decline curve: a ~0.1%/yr relative drawdown of the atmosphere's ~1.2×10¹⁵-tonne oxygen inventory (~1,000 per meg/yr, ~50× the fossil-fuel baseline) that the global O₂ budget cannot close — a gigaton-per-year sink producing no CO₂ — compounding toward the pO₂ at which both flames and highland lungs fail.
- Mirror — Esteban Duarte, her husband, a pulmonologist in El Alto, whose clinic (and then whose own blood-oxygen) turns the abstract curve into a reading on a pulse-oximeter — and who becomes the loudest voice for the war Noemi has come to doubt, because at altitude his patients suffocate first.

**Shape** — Single POV, close third on Noemi; the forensic mind is the throughline. Past tense. Chapter heads carry the instrument record — the quarterly O₂ figure in per meg (falling), the unclosed mass-balance residual in Gt/yr, the projected threshold-crossing date, and the market price of medical O₂ (rising): two numbers converging. ~120,000 words, ~33 chapters, three parts — *The Missing Sink / Prior Title / The Bellows*.

**The novum's price**
- Flesh — reaching the sink means drilling and diving into a lethal environment (the >120 °C biosphere boundary, pressure, anoxia); people die at the face to bring back cell counts. And the drawdown is written on flesh: first Esteban's patients, then Esteban, then Noemi's own SpO₂ at altitude — the decimal made a pulse.
- Money & a marriage — as air becomes a priced commodity, the sink-hunt is defunded in favor of stockpiling; Noemi spends institutional credibility and her marriage to keep the forensics alive past the point the world wants an answer it cannot sell.
- Time & clean hands — the inventory does not return on human timescales, so every year spent proving the mechanism is unrecoverable; and the counterstrike, once fired, costs the species its clean-hands position for good — to keep breathing it must burn the planet's original owner a second time.

**Science memo pointers** — O₂ monitoring: the Scripps O₂/N₂ program (Keeling), δ(O₂/N₂) in per meg, atmospheric potential oxygen, oxidative ratios, interferometric/paramagnetic/fuel-cell analyzers and the flask network — the ~1-per-meg precision that makes the fall unmistakable to instruments and invisible to lungs. Global O₂/carbon budget: GPP/respiration fluxes, the ~30 Gt/yr fossil O₂ draw, combustion/photosynthesis O₂:CO₂ stoichiometry, the "missing sink" logic inverted for O₂ — the RA must pin the sink's redox chemistry (what accepts the electrons, where the product hides, why no CO₂). Deep biosphere: SLiMEs, chemolithotrophs, IODP drilling (Chikyū, riser/riserless), cell counts and zeptowatt maintenance metabolism, geological dormancy. Deep time: the Great Oxidation Event (~2.4 Ga) and the anaerobes' "oxygen catastrophe," Permian–Triassic euxinia and the Siberian Traps, banded iron formations, sulfur MIF. Paleo-O₂ proxies: charcoal/inertinite (fire needs >~16% O₂), GEOCARBSULF/COPSE Phanerozoic models, ice-core δ(O₂/N₂). Access engineering: Kola-class ultra-deep boring, temperature/pressure limits, tracer-bead contamination control. The counterstrike's honest constraint: the "oxygen engine" is net-positive only via organic-carbon *burial*; industrial O₂ at gigaton scale is thermodynamically impossible, so the Bellows must run through biology and geology — iron fertilization, enhanced burial — partial, generational, forced to choose whom to save. Physiology: the O₂–hemoglobin dissociation curve, hypoxic hypoxia, chronic mountain sickness, hypoxic pulmonary vasoconstriction, and why highland (El Alto ~4,150 m, pO₂ ~96 mmHg) and respiratory-compromised populations cross first; combustion's limiting oxygen concentration for industry. Political economy: mirror the carbon markets — cryogenic ASU/PSA supply chains, hoarding, air rights.

**Opening image candidate** — She cracked the night's flask off the manifold, ran it through the analyzer twice, and wrote the per-meg figure in the book before she let herself set it beside the number from the year before.

**Ending directive** — An irreversible act of the hands that commits, or refuses to commit, the species to the war — a valve opened, a switch thrown, a sample sealed away, a figure logged — legible at once as the defense of a stolen home and the first shot of an extermination, without ever spoken to which. End on the gesture at the threshold; the claimant is never rendered.

**Forbidden**
- The sink as monster, hive-mind, or waking god — no face, no message, no "it noticed." It is a metabolism, read only in gas ratios and cell counts. (Guards against the Bathypelagic waking-god register.)
- The Anthropocene morality play — humanity punished for its carbon sins, Gaia's revenge as a lesson. The claimant has no verdict; it is a competitor with prior title, not karma.
- The disaster-movie register — cities gasping, mask heroics, a suffocation countdown set-piece. The apocalypse is procedural and slow: a decimal, a market, a clinic. Dread lives in the checklist, not the spectacle.
- The techno-triumphalist fix — a machine that simply manufactures air and saves everyone. The thermodynamics forbid it; the counterstrike stays partial, biological, and morally filthy.
- The whodunit — a rogue lab, a bioweapon, a human conspiracy behind the drawdown. The trail must point past every human agency into deep time and stay there.

**Levers**
- (a) POV/structure — braid a second strand: a subsurface-microbiologist POV who makes contact at the drill face, or an interchapter chorus of market tapes, ration notices, and clinic logs pricing the air.
- (b) darker/warmer — darker: the Bellows works only by re-committing the original atrocity, and Noemi throws the switch knowing it; warmer: humanity forgoes the war and chooses to live thinner — retreat from altitude, adapt to lower pO₂, a partial-pressure armistice with an owner that cannot be negotiated with.
- (c) scope — compress to one sink-hunt expedition / a single drilling season for clock; or span the drawdown's decades to the threshold-crossing, a generational chronicle where children are born to thinner air.
