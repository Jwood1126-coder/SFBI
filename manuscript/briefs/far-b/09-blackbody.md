# Nº 09 — Blackbody · FAR (Tier B)

**Secretly about** — the moral arithmetic of occupancy: a people's survival has been, unwittingly, another mind's slow apocalypse — and what the keeper of the fire surrenders to become a host, not a heat source.

**Sibling rule** — Nº 11 *Bathypelagic*: both a buried science team and a slow, non-anthropomorphic mind their instruments perturb. Split on contact — *Bathypelagic* forbids legible recognition to the last page (the gulf is the horror); *Blackbody* achieves it and pays, a costly non-linguistic negotiation in heat. Stay unknowable and it is *Bathypelagic*. (Nº 07 *Terminator Line*, the setting-twin, is split from its side: no second mind, empty furnace.)

**The two questions**
- Outward — How does a colony living on one irreplaceable heat source survive when that source becomes an ecosystem's destination — every exchanger fouled, no way to move it, no sterilization that isn't suicide?
- Inward — What must the fire's keeper become to stop guarding it as property and answer for what her people's warmth already cost a world they certified dead?

**Dyad**
- Equation — a heat-rejection budget collapsing: the reactor must shed a fixed load to the 40 K crust to hold the gradient power and life support need; biofouling drives overall U down a decay curve and reject-side temperature up, toward the scram that freezes the colony. The clock is a falling U.
- Mirror — Edith Fenwick, who signed the "dead" certification sixty years ago, now dying in a heated infirmary that is itself part of the beacon; keeping her alive draws the migration deeper in. Her verdict is the debt Reyhan inherited with the post.

**Shape** — 3rd limited, single POV (Reyhan Ashby), past tense. Chapters headed by the rejection ledger — thermal output, heat shed vs. required, overall U (falling), reject-side °C (rising), margin to scram, migration-front depth off the seismic array. ~115,000 words, ~36 chapters, three parts by migration stage: Detection / Fouling / Hearth.

**The novum's price**
- Flesh — clearing the colonized exchangers is hot manual work at the crust interface; a de-fouling crew is scalded or maimed by a steam-purge.
- A relationship priced in watts — Edith's warm sickroom is a beacon; the comfort keeping the founder alive feeds the vent-slow into the habitat, and Reyhan rations a dying woman's heat against the colony's.
- Money/future — the only settlement is a permanent hearth-tax: a fixed fraction of irreplaceable power dedicated forever to the guests, subtracted from the next generation's growth.

**Science memo pointers** — Rogue / free-floating planets (OGLE microlensing; Stevenson 1999). Fix the tidal-heat source: Steppenwolf as an eccentric-orbit moon of a free-floating giant (Io/Europa dissipation, k₂/Q, W/m²) or a binary-planet flex; a 40 K surface radiating to ~3 K space; N₂/CO/CH₄/O₂ ices. Geothermal power and the Carnot ceiling of ~350 K/40 K; enhanced geothermal systems; heat exchangers (LMTD, overall U, fouling factors), condenser biofouling as the U-collapse failure. Closed-loop ECLSS, thermal loop the fragile one. Gradient metabolism — thermosynthesis (Muller), dissipative structures (Prigogine), vent chemolithotrophy; Arrhenius kinetics at cryogenic temperature, why cognition runs in months. Survey error: far-IR biosignatures against an optical-chemical band. The channel is literal blackbody physics — Planck, Wien, Stefan-Boltzmann; pulsework modulates reject surfaces in temperature amplitude and pulse width. Seismics: geophones, tiltmeters, telling a migration front from tidal creep. Numbers: MW thermal, tidal flux, U and fouling factors, Carnot efficiency, Arrhenius rates 40–250 K, Planck radiance.

**Opening image candidate** — At the number-three tap she chalked the reject temperature against the morning's, found it a tenth warm, and ran her bared palm down the exchanger to feel the softness the gauge had not yet learned to name.

**Ending directive** — Close on Reyhan's hand at the rejection controls, setting the Hearth's steady, permanent output and logging the colony's forever-reduced margin — one pulse sent into the crust — whether it is now a host or only a fire that agreed to burn lower left in the act, never in speech.

**Forbidden**
- The ecology unknowable to the end — cosmic-horror silence, no legible contact; that is *Bathypelagic*.
- The negotiation as language — decoded message, handshake, telepathy; meaning stays thermodynamic, carried only in modulated heat.
- Sterilization as a clean option merely refused on morals; the purge must also be suicidal, collapsing the gradient the colony now needs.
- The waking-god superorganism to commune with; keep it distributed, dumb in its parts, organized only statistically.
- The pastoral hug and the humans-the-villains sermon; coexistence is a resented, permanent tax, the verdict-error a tragedy, not a lecture.

**Levers** — (a) dual POV: Reyhan against a xenologist who would understand before the engineers purge; or interleave Fenwick's old survey as found-text against the arrival it licensed. (b) darker: the migration is a dying vanguard fleeing a cooling vent, coexistence becomes unaffordable triage; warmer: the vent-slow tend the exchangers better than human hands, bending toward unasked symbiosis. (c) scope: compress to one fouling-to-scram crisis for clock, or span the sixty years for meaning.
