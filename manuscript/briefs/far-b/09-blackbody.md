# Nº 09 — Blackbody · FAR (Tier B)

**Secretly about** — the moral arithmetic of occupancy: a people's survival has been, unknowing, another mind's slow apocalypse — and what the fire's keeper must surrender to become a host, not just a heat source.

**Sibling rule** — Nº 11 *Bathypelagic*: both bury a science team beside a vast, non-anthropomorphic mind their instruments perturb. Split on contact — *Bathypelagic* denies legible recognition to the last page, the gulf itself the horror; *Blackbody* achieves it and pays, in a wordless negotiation carried in heat. Stay unknowable and it is only *Bathypelagic* underground.

**The two questions**
- Outward — How does a colony on one irreplaceable heat source survive when that source becomes an ecosystem's destination — every exchanger fouled, the reactor unmovable, no sterilization that isn't suicide?
- Inward — What must the fire's keeper become to stop guarding it as property and answer for what her people's warmth already cost a world they certified dead?

**Dyad**
- Equation — a heat-rejection budget collapsing: the reactor must shed a fixed load into the 40 K crust to hold the gradient power and life support draw on; biofouling drives overall U down a decay curve and reject-side temperature up, toward the scram that freezes the colony. The clock is a falling U.
- Mirror — Edith Fenwick, who signed the "dead" certification sixty years ago, now dying in a heated infirmary that is itself part of the beacon: keeping her warm draws the migration deeper. Her verdict is the debt Reyhan inherited with the post.

**Shape** — Close third, single POV (Reyhan Ashby), past tense. Chapters headed by the rejection ledger — heat shed vs. required, overall U (falling), reject-side °C (rising), margin to scram, migration-front depth off the seismic array. ~115,000 words, ~36 chapters; three parts by migration stage: Detection / Fouling / Hearth.

**The novum's price**
- Flesh — clearing colonized exchangers is hot manual labor at the crust interface; a de-fouling crew is scalded when a steam-purge lets go.
- A relationship priced in watts — Edith's warm sickroom is a beacon; the comfort keeping the founder alive feeds the vent-slow into the habitat, and Reyhan rations a dying woman's heat against the colony's.
- Money/future — the only settlement is a permanent hearth-tax: a fixed fraction of irreplaceable power dedicated forever to the guests, subtracted from the next generation's growth.

**Science memo pointers** — Rogue / free-floating planets (OGLE microlensing; Stevenson 1999; Abbot & Switzer 2011, the literal "Steppenwolf planet"). Fix the tidal-heat source or the book fails an engineer: Steppenwolf as an eccentric-orbit moon of a free-floating giant (Io/Europa dissipation, k₂/Q) or a binary flex; 40 K surface radiating to ~3 K; N₂/CO/CH₄ ices. Geothermal power against the Carnot ceiling ~350 K/40 K; enhanced geothermal systems; heat exchangers (LMTD, overall U, fouling factors), condenser biofouling as the U-collapse mode. Closed-loop ECLSS, thermal loop the fragile one. Gradient metabolism — thermosynthesis (Muller), dissipative structures (Prigogine), vent chemolithotrophy; Arrhenius kinetics 40–250 K, why cognition runs in months. Survey error: far-IR biosignatures missed by an optical-chemical band. The channel is literal blackbody physics — Planck, Wien, Stefan-Boltzmann; pulsework modulates reject surfaces in amplitude and pulse width. Seismics: geophones, tiltmeters, telling a migration front from tidal creep.

**Opening image candidate** — At the number-three tap she chalked the reject temperature against the morning's, found it a tenth warm, and ran her bared palm down the exchanger to feel the softness the gauge had not yet learned to name.

**Ending directive** — Close on Reyhan's hand at the rejection controls, setting the Hearth's steady, permanent output and logging the colony's forever-reduced margin — one pulse sent down into the crust. Whether she has made a host or only a fire that agreed to burn lower must live in the act, never in speech.

**Forbidden**
- The ecology unknowable to the end — cosmic-horror silence, no legible contact; that is *Bathypelagic*.
- The negotiation as language — decoded message, handshake, telepathy; meaning stays thermodynamic, carried only in modulated heat.
- Sterilization as a clean option merely refused on principle; the purge must also be suicidal, collapsing the gradient the colony now needs.
- The waking-god superorganism to commune with; keep it distributed, dumb in its parts, organized only statistically.
- The pastoral hug and the humans-are-the-villains sermon; coexistence is a resented, permanent tax, the verdict-error a tragedy.

**Levers** — (a) Dual POV: pair Reyhan with a xenologist who would understand before the engineers purge, or interleave Fenwick's old survey as found-text against the arrival it licensed. (b) Darker: the migration is a dying vanguard fleeing a cooling vent, coexistence unaffordable triage; warmer: the vent-slow tend the exchangers better than human hands, bending toward unasked symbiosis. (c) Scope: compress to one fouling-to-scram crisis, or span the sixty years.
