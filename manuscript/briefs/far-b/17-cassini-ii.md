# Nº 17 — Cassini II · FAR (Tier B)

**Secretly about** — the seduction of completeness: that to finish a text is to consent to it, and the deepest discipline for two people whose craft and marriage is reading is to leave the last stratum closed.

**Sibling rule** — Most in danger of resembling **Nº 04 Overburden** — same ice-shell ocean world, same inhuman machinery stirring under the ice. Split on legibility: Overburden's ocean must stay unreadable and motiveless (working under a will you cannot parse); Cassini II's is authored, decoded, and understood — the dread is that comprehension *works*, and the reading is the waking.

**The two questions**
- Outward — how do a handful of specialists, eighty light-minutes from help, pull a civilization's knowledge from a medium that heats as they read it, before consumables and the launch window run out?
- Inward — what must a reader become to shut a book one grain short of the end, to un-want the answer?

**Dyad**
- Equation — each decoded stratum advances the restore, and the restore is exothermic: the ocean's temperature and the crew's departure window are one curve read from opposite ends, and past a cumulative threshold the boot self-sustains and cannot be aborted.
- Mirror — the linguist and the cryptographer, married to the work and to each other, whose shared life *is* finishing texts — and now one of them must want to stop.

**Shape** — Dual limited third, past tense — two POV, Marceau (meaning) and Vantis (structure), alternating. Device: chapters head with the read-log — grain-sequence designations and decode indices — and each of three parts opens on the standing thermal figure, "the Warm," risen. ~120,000 words, ~34 chapters, three parts.

**The novum's price**
- **Flesh/time:** structure survives only at cryo — every read is a cassette held near 80 K, gloved handling shift on shift, cold injuries and weeks lost to annealed, ruined grains.
- **Mass/money:** intact lattices demand slow plume transits, not flybys — delta-v and consumables burned to hover in a geyser, each transit priced against the ride home.
- **Relationship:** the reading order that decodes fastest is the boot sequence; to protect each other, the pair must sabotage the one act — finishing the text — their partnership was built on.

**Science memo pointers** — Enceladus plume: south-polar tiger-stripe jets; INMS/CDA detections (H2O, CO2, CH4, NH3, H2, NaCl salts, silica nanograins → seafloor hydrothermal vents >90 °C; HCN and phosphates from the 2023 reanalyses). Sampling: hypervelocity capture ionizes and shatters nanostructure (Stardust/aerogel limits) — hence slow-transit capture, cryo-preservation, sublimation/annealing above ~100 K, COSPAR planetary protection. Reading structure: cryo-EM, X-ray/electron diffraction, atom-probe tomography. Decipherment: Shannon entropy, undeciphered-script method (Linear A, Indus, rongorongo), cryptanalysis, error-correcting codes; DNA data storage (Church, Goldman, Erlich) as the working analog — addressing, redundancy, destructive reads. Restore: autocatalysis and von Neumann self-construction bounded by Enceladus's ~5 GW tidal/radiogenic heat budget, not free energy. Environment: Saturn's magnetosphere (milder than Jupiter's — load-bearing against a Europa setting), E-ring dust, ~70–90-minute one-way lag, DSN scheduling. Price honestly: pareidolia, the look-elsewhere effect, self-contamination — their own organics read back as signal.

**Opening image candidate** — Marceau seats the shift's cassette in the cryostage, holds it at eighty kelvin while the diffractometer walks the first grain, and the pattern that resolves is not the six-fold of ice but a ruled thing, addressed.

**Ending directive** — End on a physical act at the read-console or the sample cassette — a sequence begun, halted, or deliberately mis-ordered — and one logged line; do not narrate what wakes, or fails to.

**Forbidden**
- The makers shown, heard, or given a face or a plain-spoken message to the future.
- The waking rendered as a monster attacking the ship — an eruption, a chase, a moon that cracks.
- Decipherment written as rapture or psychedelia instead of earned, procedural reading — no lone-genius epiphany.
- The panspermia reveal: the archive is Earth's ancestors, or "the restore is us."
- The nuke-the-moon climax as the only available move.

**Levers**
- (a) Collapse to Vantis's single POV, structure-first — the code withheld from the reader as long as the sense is withheld from him.
- (b) Warmer: the archive is a curated seedbank, not owners; restoration is gardening, and the horror is choosing what not to grow.
- (c) Scope out to Earth: the grains are beamed home, and the real fight — keep reading or stop — happens eighty light-minutes and an institution away from the people at risk.
