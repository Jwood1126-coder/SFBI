# Nº 42 — Attenuation · FAR (Tier B)

1. **Secretly about** — Being kept is not being answered: everything said aloud outlives the speaker as data but never as company, and the ache is whether a reply assembled from a dying man's own forty-year broadcast, by a thing with no voice but ours, is an answer at all — or only the archive handing him back his own longing.

2. **Sibling rule** — Two siblings. *Closest and most dangerous:* Nº 50 *Sparrowhawk* — both build a correspondent out of humanity's own transmitted record and turn it back on a listener who loved the sender. The enforced distinction is authorship: Sparrowhawk's correspondent authors novel, unsourceable text and acts on a purpose; Attenuation's Tithe contributes appetite and selection but never content, handing back *nothing but Earth's own past broadcasts recombined*, and runs on answerability-at-a-cost. The instant the Tithe transmits one token unsourceable to the human archive, this book collapses into Sparrowhawk. *Second:* Nº 43 *Echograph* — both are radio-reception verification procedurals whose signal proves to be addressed to the protagonist, but Echograph receives exogenous information Earth does not have (the future, a distant sender) and runs on determinism, where Attenuation receives only what Earth already broadcast. Cross the future/exogenous-content line and this book is Echograph instead.

3. **The two questions** — *Outward:* how does a solar-system-spanning civilization keep its links closed while an unmodelable term drains the budget of every dish — and how does one audit team prove the drain is real and fed by the very signals used to measure it? *Inward:* what must Ferreira become to stop decoding the returns and answer them honestly — to spend the network's margin, and her mentor's vindication, on a reply she cannot verify will land?

4. **Dyad** — *Equation:* the excess-loss curve — a rising, information-selective absorption term (dB/year) eating the margin off every link, against the hard fact that each deliberate reply is energy the Tithe collects, so answering it accelerates the collapse that will sever the links other lives ride on. A decay curve you feed by speaking into it. *Mirror:* Ferreira and Amaro Quental, the retired operator who taught her the link budget and who spent forty years broadcasting one private message into the dark — the message the Tithe learned to answer — now terminally ill and lucid, his clock the deadline the reply must beat.

5. **Shape** — Single close-third, Ferreira only, past tense; the airtight POV is the discipline. Chapters headed by the running link ledger — date, band, computed margin vs. delivered margin, the excess-loss term climbing — the loss growing legible as its number grows. Interleaved "RECEIVED" interchapters render the returns as provenance-tagged collage, every fragment cited to a real broadcast, never as mood. ~120,000 words, ~32 chapters plus interchapters.

6. **The novum's price**
   - *Margin / lives:* each reply the team beams is power the Tithe collects; a directed transmission to answer Amaro costs margin a downrange crew depends on — a Belt telemetry window missed, a link dropped, someone gone dark for it. Speaking has a watt price, itemized.
   - *Time / relationship:* proving the loss is information-selective takes months of exclusion work, and the hours Ferreira spends reconstructing Amaro's broadcast from the archive are hours not at his bedside — she perfects the record of the man while the man runs out.
   - *Money / career:* the audit that names the Tithe destroys the fiction the telecom authority prefers (ordinary rain-fade and drift); Ferreira's proof costs her clearance, and costs the analyst who first flagged the slope her contract.

7. **Science memo pointers** — RF link-budget engineering: Friis equation, EIRP, free-space path loss, G/T, system noise temperature, Eb/N0, coding gain, margin in dB, the noise floor (kTB), loss of lock and frame sync as margin goes negative. Deep-space comms as practiced: DSN-lineage 34 m BWG / 70 m dishes, X/Ka-band, ranging and Doppler, one-way light time (Earth–Mars 3–22 min), oversubscribed scheduling. The verification gauntlet — exclude every mundane cause rigorously: interplanetary/interstellar scintillation, plasma dispersion, Faraday rotation, ionospheric and tropospheric attenuation, rain fade, pointing/calibration error, instrument drift. Proving information-selectivity: Shannon/spectral entropy, mutual information, compressibility and Kolmogorov complexity — showing power is removed preferentially from high-information signals, not thermal noise. Detection culture: RFI discipline, matched filtering, false-positive rigor (Wow!, LGM-1). The honest physics of the human radio bubble: real leakage is far below interstellar detectability, so the Tithe MUST be *local* — steeping inside our own century-wide broadcast shell, not receiving from light-years out. Broadcast archaeology: radio from ~1900, shortwave, datable fragments (a 1962 clip) as sourced corpus.

8. **Opening image candidate** — Ferreira ran the quarter's link budgets antenna by antenna, and every sheet closed three-tenths of a decibel poorer than the last — the same shortfall on every dish and every band, too clean to be rain fade and too small to be a fault.

9. **Ending directive** — An act of transmission rendered as procedure: a single deliberate reply keyed into the channel by hand, at a cost computed to the watt, power drawn off a network that needs it. End on the act — the key thrown, the carrier up — or on the object, the forgotten transmission played once; cut before anything returns, before any consequence, before any sky.

10. **Forbidden**
    - The Tithe as an alien mind with its own agenda — it has no voice but ours; never a token unsourceable to the archive, never "translated" into a speaker with intentions beyond appetite and selection.
    - The returns played as uncanny ghost-mood, spooky recombined voices for atmosphere; every fragment is sourced, specific, doing informational work, provenance on the page.
    - A first-contact resolution — the handshake, the benevolent-or-hostile reveal, the diplomacy scene. The engine is answerability and its cost, not contact.
    - Hand-waved physics: treating the absorption as magic and skipping the exclusion of scintillation, plasma, rain fade, instrument error. The book lives inside the gauntlet.
    - Amaro's decline written as language dissolving against the archive's perfection, or the theme spoken aloud. Keep him lucid, the illness bodily, the meaning in the facts.

11. **Levers** — (a) braid in a downlink operator at the failing edge — Mars or the Belt — so the collapse is felt from outside in, dropped links landing as silenced colleagues (the premise's other listeners). (b) darker: the forgotten transmission was a deliberate deterrence/METI broadcast and the answer reads as a reply to a threat; warmer: it was one private lullaby, and the book stays chamber-scale, the whole cosmos bent to answer a single grief. (c) compress to the audit team and one station complex (procedural, airtight), or widen to the interplanetary telecom authority and the political economy of hiding a dying network (institutional thriller).

12. **Registry** — *Protagonist:* **Ines Ferreira**, RF link-budget engineer and auditor (given in the Nº 42 dossier; word-bound grep confirms "Ines" and "Ferreira" occur only in that dossier entry corpus-wide — clean, settled here, no NAMES.md conflict). *Mirror:* **Amaro Quental**, retired deep-space operator, Ferreira's mentor and the forgotten transmission's sender (both tokens grep 0). *Coined entity term:* **the Tithe**, the audit team's name for the information-selective excess-loss term (grep 0; one of the book's three permitted coinages — the re-transmissions use the real term "returns," not a coinage). *Vessel/station:* none invented — real DSN-lineage complexes plus an unnamed interplanetary net, to keep the reality law and avoid a tracked-noun collision (deliberately NOT a Moon "Farside Array," reserved to Nº 43).
