# Nº 03 — The Greenhouse Variable · FAR (Tier B)

**Secretly about** — custodianship that outlives its purpose: a keeper deciding who the world's inheritance belongs to when the heirs are dead and only strangers are coming to claim it.

**Sibling rule** — Nº 05 *Perennial*. Both are lone woman-biologists watching engineered life repurpose toward an unplanned fixation metabolism. Enforce the one split: Perennial's antagonist is authorless emergence, "no malice at all"; Greenhouse's is deliberate design with an author inbound. If the emptying ever reads as accident, or the sky stays empty, it becomes Perennial.

**The two questions**
- Outward — Can one botanist keep ten thousand species and herself alive on a station falling out of the sky, long enough to read what emptied the world and land the seed before the vault burns?
- Inward — What must she become, steward or judge or midwife, to decide whether a planet cleared for replanting is still humanity's to withhold or hand on?

**Dyad**
- Equation — Svalbard-2 decays without reboost; her finite reaction mass is one budget split between staying up for the arrival and coming down to deliver seed — spend it one way, forfeit the other — as the inbound's deceleration ETA falls from past Jupiter.
- Mirror — Silas Okoro, a farmer on amateur radio: the last living voice below, whose ground she watches turn impossibly green from orbit even as the greening starves him, because the payload feeds no crop of ours.

**Shape** — Single POV, close third on Amara; solitude is the point. Past tense. Chapter heads carry live telemetry — altitude (km), days-to-reentry, solar flux F10.7, the inbound's range and ETA — two numbers falling toward each other. ~100,000 words, ~30 chapters.

**The novum's price**
- Proving the payload engineered means germinating and infecting real accessions — burning irreplaceable seed lines to read the thing killing the world; every assay is a species read to death.
- Every experiment, slew, and radio pass spends power and reaction mass the station cannot renew; studying the arrival shortens how long the vault stays up.
- The fixation bloom that resurrects the soil is inedible to humans; keeping the seed means she cannot also keep Silas.

**Science memo pointers**
- N-fixation biochemistry: nitrogenase and the *nif* cluster, its O₂ poisoning, ~16 ATP/N₂, diazotrophs, engineered-cereal-symbiosis work (Pivot Bio-family) — the novum must survive a biochemist.
- Synthetic-genome forensics: codon usage, GC skew, restriction-site spacing, molecular-clock phylogenetics, watermarked synthetic DNA (Venter).
- Orbital mechanics: LEO ~400 km, drag, ballistic coefficient, thermospheric density via F10.7/Kp, decay-without-reboost timescales (months to ~2 yr per solar cycle), reboost/deorbit Δv, CMG saturation, reentry heating, sample-return-capsule deorbit.
- Bioregenerative ECLSS: water recovery, CO₂ scrubbing (CDRA/Sabatier), O₂ electrolysis, bioregenerative loops (MELiSSA, ISS Veggie/APH), single-operator failure modes.
- Detecting a braking craft: IR-excess and plume signatures, optical astrometry and occultation from a lone platform, interstellar deceleration Δv; the dying DSN.
- Seed science: orthodox vs. recalcitrant seed, cryostorage, viability/germination testing, accessions, real Svalbard/CGIAR practice; LEO radiation (GCR/SPE) as seed mutagen and electronics-SEU source.

**Opening image candidate** — She lifted each sprouted grain off the wet blotter with forceps, counted radicles under the loupe, and logged nineteen viable of twenty before the fixed-hour comms pass.

**Ending directive** — An irreversible act of her hands on the vault's contents — a planting, a release, a withholding, a deorbit — that answers what a seed vault is for; whether the arrival treats her as partner or weed must be legible in the act, never spoken, never met on the page.

**Forbidden**
- Rendering the gardeners: no faces, voices, ships, or motives; the book ends at contact's threshold or before.
- A human mastermind — rogue lab, cult, whodunit reveal. The evidence points past every human lab; keep it there.
- The Crusoe-in-orbit breakdown: hallucinated companions, madness montage, the volleyball. Her competence is the plot.
- Hand-waved biochemistry no biochemist would sign; the O₂ problem and the energy budget must be paid.
- The greening resolving to pure hope or pure horror; it stays at once resurrection and eviction.

**Levers**
- (a) Braid a second asynchronous strand — Silas's ground, or her logs addressed to a dead audience.
- (b) Warmer: she midwifes the hybrid world for its successors. Darker: she deorbits the vault rather than hand its new owners the planet's genome.
- (c) Scope: compress to the single reentry window (days, near real-time) for clock, or the multi-year approach ending at contact for meaning.
