# Nº 15 — Light Curve · FAR (Tier B)

1. **Secretly about** — Being noticed is not being loved: a woman who spent her career wanting the universe to answer receives its one reply — an order to go dark — and must learn that stewardship, and love, can mean silence.

2. **Sibling rule** — Nº 14 *The Last Lighthouse*. Light Curve is a *decipherment* novel — the signal resolves into meaning and the terror is comprehension; The Last Lighthouse stays pure *detection*, the hunter never decoded, the monster a graph. If Nº 15's message never becomes words, it collapses into its sibling.

3. **The two questions** — *Outward:* how does a small team confirm, against every incentive to be wrong, that a star's death was engineered, and decide what a species does with a warning it can neither answer safely nor un-hear? *Inward:* what must a lifelong listener become to choose silence — to hold a truth she cannot act on and love people by not telling them all of it?

4. **Dyad** — *Equation:* the front's velocity and vector, fit from a dozen dated historic supernovae, yields an arrival schedule for the nearest un-killed star — against a light-cone ledger proving every choice concerns events already decided, and that our own century-wide radio-leakage sphere already crossed the mapped path. *Mirror:* Carmen and her mother Pilar, whose progressive aphasia strips syntax from a living person in the next room while Carmen recovers it from a dead star's.

5. **Shape** — Single close-third, Carmen only, past tense; the airtight POV is the plankton's-eye discipline. Three parts by decode stage (anomaly, syntax, semantics); chapters headed by pipeline log lines — MJD, filter, magnitude, running entropy — legibility climbing as the numbers do. ~115,000 words, ~30 chapters.

6. **The novum's price**
   - Years of telescope time chasing dust-scattered *light echoes* to re-observe centuries-old supernovae, a career staked on a signature that could be systematics — the professional death of the astronomer who found faces in noise.
   - The argument for silence costs her Xavier, who wants to publish and answer, and costs the honesty she owes Pilar — the one person she cannot tell the thing that ate her life.
   - The neutrino re-processing costs petabytes and forces five rival detector collaborations into a secrecy pact none wants; the analyst who first flagged the fine structure burns out holding it.

7. **Science memo pointers** — Type II-P light curves (shock breakout, ~100-day recombination plateau, Co-56 tail, bolometric correction); Betelgeuse as red supergiant ~168 pc, uncertain distance, the 2019 Great Dimming as precedent. Time-domain photometry (Johnson-Cousins/SDSS/Rubin bands, differential photometry, red noise, bright-source saturation, AAVSO) on Rubin/LSST, ZTF, ASAS-SN, ATLAS. Neutrino astrophysics: ~3×10⁵³ erg, ~10⁵⁸ ν in ~10 s; SN 1987A's ~25 events at 50 kpc vs millions at 0.2 kpc; water-Cherenkov/IBD (Super-K, Hyper-K), liquid-argon (DUNE), scintillator (JUNO), IceCube rate rise, CEvNS, SNEWS 2.0; ν precede optical breakout by hours, and detector deadtime/buffer decide whether fine structure survives. Decipherment: Shannon/conditional entropy, mutual information, Zipf-Mandelbrot, Kolmogorov complexity, IAA post-detection, METI/anticryptography (Lincos); failure modes — pareidolia, look-elsewhere, trials factors, aliasing, LGM-1 and Wow!. Archaeology: light echoes (Tycho 1572, Cas A, SN 1987A), Harvard DASCH plates, historic SNe along the Local/Orion Arm, extinction maps, Antares as candidate target. Rigor: blind analysis, pre-registration, systematics budgets, 3σ vs 5σ.

8. **Opening image candidate** — Carmen slowed the ten seconds of neutrino count to a ten-thousandth of real time and let the star take all afternoon to die, marking by hand each step in the rate the trigger had been too coarse to keep.

9. **Ending directive** — An act of withholding rendered as procedure: a reply composed and not sent, a switch left unthrown, a single word taught to someone who will forget it. End on the object or the unmade motion — before any consequence, before any sky.

10. **Forbidden**
    - The senders shown, named, or explained; any on-page war, ship, or body — the war stays inference, the enemy a syntax.
    - The decode delivered as a subtitled paragraph of alien speech; meaning arrives through what the team does, never an info-dump.
    - The dying-mother/dying-star rhyme played for sentiment — no deathbed epiphany, no metaphor spoken aloud; render the aphasia clinically.
    - Government/military thriller machinery; institutions arrive as embargoes, memos, telescope-time allocations.
    - The heroic-broadcast uplift ending or its nihilist mirror; the choice stays small, procedural, undecided, the doubt held long.

11. **Levers** — (a) braid in the grad-student analyst's POV, or an interchapter chorus of the 1572/1604/1987A observers who recorded the signature blind. (b) darker: the muzzle-flash reading wins — it is bait, and answering is what something wants — or the next kill is already struck, its light decades out; warmer: the message resolves smaller and kinder, a keeper marking a shoal, and the book becomes custody, not dread. (c) compress to one season and one detector, or expand to a multi-decade vigil in which Carmen dies mid-watch losing her own words, inverting the mirror onto her heir.
