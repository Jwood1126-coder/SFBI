# Nº 33 — Shade · FAR (Tier B)

1. **Secretly about** — the courage it takes to touch the controls of a catastrophe you inherited but did not cause, and the harder courage to accept that keeping your world lit means letting it be found.

2. **Sibling rule** — Nº 24 *Under the Veil*. Both dim Earth under an engineered shield reached by working an old control system whose use exposes us to something outside. Enforce the authorship split: Shade's shade is *alien wreckage that answers back* — the controls are a live inheritance, not maintenance, and being-seen is *active address* to a shatterer the plot can wake. If the shard ever reads as a thing we built, or as inert infrastructure we merely tend rather than fly, it has become *Under the Veil*.

3. **The two questions** — *Outward:* how do a starving planet and a small crew on a tumbling four-hundred-thousand-kilometer sail null its residual spin and steer it off the Earth–Sun line — using the dead makers' own actuators — before it locks into permanent shadow and the growing seasons pass a threshold no thaw can undo? *Inward:* what must they become to take a wheel they did not build and accept authorship of a fix that names their species to whatever ended its makers?

4. **Dyad** — *Equation:* the shard's tumble damps on a known decay curve toward a stable Earth-shadowing attitude; the crew has until it locks to null its angular momentum and re-point it, bounded by the ancient thrusters' finite reaction mass and the film's tear tolerance — run against Earth's own clock, insolation already down 4%, degree-days bleeding off, grain carryover in months, sea-ice albedo nearing a tip past which removing the shade restores nothing in time. *Mirror:* Tuva aloft and her daughter Sena below — Sena's failing harvest is the number the maneuver is really about, and the strain of a mother who left the dying world to go tend a dead one's machine.

5. **Shape** — Dual close-third, past tense: Tuva on the Leaf, Sena on the ground — the premise's own split made structural, and the split from the single-POV sibling. Chapters headed by two coupled telemetry lines — Earth surface insolation / degree-day ledger, and the shard's attitude state (tumble rate, pointing error, reaction mass remaining) — the two clocks that must be reconciled and may not be. Four parts by mission phase against the dimming's stages. ~125,000 words, ~34 chapters.

6. **The novum's price**
   - *Flesh & consumables:* the control nodes lie kilometers apart across a sun-facing membrane that is a lethal thermal, charge, and radiation environment; reaching them spends EVA water, scrubber, battery, tether — and because the actuators *still respond*, a crewmember is maimed or killed when a node fires under them. Pay it in a hand, an eye, a life.
   - *Mass & irreversibility:* nulling the spin means spending irreplaceable reaction mass and stressing the film — every correction risks propagating a tear no one can take back, less shade bought at the cost of controllability. Plus months of transit and traverse charged against Earth's degree-days.
   - *Relationship & species:* Sena's food-triage means choosing which regions' seed and heat survive the cold — a betrayal banked in every ration curve — while the maneuver that saves the harvest is the same act that addresses the shatterer: survival bought with visibility.

7. **Science memo pointers** — Astrodynamics of high area-to-mass-ratio bodies: solar-radiation-pressure dominance, lightness number β, characteristic acceleration, non-Keplerian equilibria / statites and displaced-orbit station-keeping sunward (McInnes; Earth–Sun L1 ≈ 1.5×10⁶ km, ~5 s one-way light-time). The load-bearing figure: reconcile the 4% dimming — solar constant 1361 W/m², 4% ≈ 54 W/m² — with occulting area, standoff, and penumbral softening (why patchy/off-axis film yields ~4%, not total). Solar-sail / thin-film engineering: aluminized polyimide membranes, areal density (sub-g to few g/m²), IKAROS, LightSail-2, NEA Scout; attitude control by reflectivity-control devices, control/tip vanes, CoM–CoP offset, angular-momentum dumping; wrinkle, billow, and tear-propagation mechanics; thermal buckling and millennia of UV/charged-particle embrittlement. Heliophysics/space weather: solar particle events, CMEs, spacecraft charging and arcing on a conductive film (the "still responds" hazard), dose (Sv) budgeting; detection via differential transit photometry (straight-edge/angular-size discrimination from sunspot or planet, absent limb-darkening). Agroclimatology (Sena's trade): insolation → growing degree days, crop base temperatures (maize 10 °C), PAR and C3/C4 yield loss; analogs — Tambora 1815, Pinatubo 1991 (~ −0.5 °C, ~ −2 to −4 W/m²), nuclear/volcanic-winter crop models; ocean thermal lag, sea-ice albedo tipping, grain carryover (months), seed-bank viability. ECLSS: closed-loop O₂/CO₂/water recovery, EVA thermal control at high flux; heavy-lift economics of a failing industrial base. Disciplines to staff: astrodynamics, solar-sail GNC, heliophysics, agronomy, ECLSS engineering, solar/transit photometry. The reality law rides the shard; the one implausibility is the makers.

8. **Opening image candidate** — Tuva measured the shard's pointing across three consecutive frames before she would let the word *settling* onto the attitude log, because nothing this dead damps its own tumble, and the Leaf had shed a half-degree of it in an hour.

9. **Ending directive** — Close on the coupled instruments: the maneuver taking on Earth's side, light returning, measured — set against one line in the shard's telemetry that no one commanded. An object, an act at a console, or a callout of that line. Leave whether it is echo or answer unresolved; no confirmed contact, no fleet, no clean sunrise.

10. **Forbidden**
    - The shatterer shown or personified — no fleet, no face, no on-page war; it stays inference in the logs and, at most, a telemetry line that should not exist.
    - The shard as a Rosetta-stone descent — the controls are learned by *operating* them, hands and procedure, never by translating a marker-ring tour of a dead civilization (guards Nº 31).
    - The book staying at the telescope — detection is a first fact, not the novel; get onto the film, or it collapses into Nº 14/15.
    - The salvage-western swarm — the shard is one moving object shadowing Earth, not a scavenger's frontier of nanomass prices (guards Nº 21).
    - Apocalypse-as-spectacle — the dimming is felt through agronomy and ration math, not senate floors or riot set-pieces; no heroic-broadcast uplift, no doomer nihilism.

11. **Levers** — (a) collapse to single airtight POV (Tuva), Earth rendered only through received data; or braid a third thread, the discoverer, as transit-light-curve interchapters. (b) *Darker:* the tear is unavoidable and the choice becomes which 4% to keep; or steering succeeds and the reply's first light arrives on the last page. *Warmer:* the watchkeeper systems are a caretaker, not a trap — steering is a maintenance protocol re-tasked, dread becoming inheritance. (c) Compress to the traverse-and-steer on the Leaf alone, near-real-time; or widen to a generational frame — Tuva reaches the shard and ages out, Sena completes the decision decades later as the climate tips, inverting mother and daughter.
