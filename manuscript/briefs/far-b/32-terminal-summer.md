# Nº 32 — Terminal Summer · FAR (Tier B)

**Secretly about** — the difference between surviving a place and belonging to it: a refugee arguing that her ice-born daughter is native, not contamination, to a universe that keeps the receipts.

**Sibling rule** — Nº 03 *The Greenhouse Variable* (engineered catastrophe; inbound makers who read us as a weed). Distinction: there the plague is aimed at us and the makers garden a cleared field; here the warming is ours and merely tripped an ancient alarm — the makers are indifferent instrument-keepers, humanity accidental contamination in a fouled experiment, not a targeted crop.

**The two questions** — *Outward:* how do two hundred million refugees build a civilization on the one continent left while an unearthed instrument counts down its makers' return? *Inward:* what must Inez become to make an accident worth keeping — to win her daughter the standing of a native, not a refugee on borrowed ground?

**Dyad** — *Equation:* the return countdown — the fixed interval from the observatory firing its alarm to the responders arriving (signal-lag plus transit, no FTL); only the season's last days of polar light are workable. *Mirror:* Inez and Nkiru, her continent-born daughter — humanity's case is argued for the child who is the ice's first native.

**Shape** — single close-third on Inez Okoye, glaciologist-turned-surveyor; past tense. Chapter heads: paleoclimate timestamps from the observatory's record, descending through deep time toward zero (−33.9 Ma… −2.6 Ma… −11,700 yr… present), then flipping in the final part to the forward arrival clock (+T). ~125,000 words, ~34 chapters, four parts.

**The novum's price**
- *Time/money:* the Basement is a hot-water-drill descent through kilometres of ice on a finite fuel-and-daylight budget; every metre is a day stolen from siting Fimbul's extension.
- *Flesh:* the structure's warmth means basal meltwater — flooding, refreezing, a cold-injury or borehole death — and the clean-access rite against fouling the site is the theme's daily flesh-cost.
- *Relationship:* proving the "finger on the trigger" implicates the ice-retreat that gave them the continent; keeping the logs open records humanity as contamination, costs Inez the settlement's trust, and gambles Nkiru's standing.

**Science memo pointers** — Radioglaciology: radio-echo sounding (~60 MHz), phase-sensitive radar (ApRES) for basal melt, BedMachine bed inversions; the warmth reads as a bright specular reflector (the subglacial-lake signature), a geothermal-flux high, local flow acceleration. Subglacial clean access: hot-water drilling, borehole refreeze, and the real forward-contamination protocols — load-bearing, since humans are the contaminant. Paleoclimate the telemetry must out-resolve: benthic δ¹⁸O (CENOGRID-Zachos), Mg/Ca as the paleo-temperature proxy (deconvolving temperature from the ice-volume signal), δ¹¹B and alkenone εₚ as the deep-time CO₂ proxies, Milankovitch forcing (405/100/41/23 kyr), the Eocene–Oligocene glaciation (~34 Ma; Drake/Tasman gateways, ACC onset), the PETM. Clathrate: methane-hydrate stability zone, dissociation under bottom-water warming, the δ¹³C excursion separating a natural trigger from a nudged one — the decade-scale "gun" is the one implausibility. Continent physics: the 35 °C wet-bulb limit emptying the tropics, ~58 m sea-level equivalent, isostatic rebound, immature cryosols, and the true limiter at 75–80 °S — photoperiod, not heat.

**Opening image candidate** — Six hours into the season's first line, the radar sled's bed-return under Inez's cursor stopped reading like rock and began reading like a room.

**Ending directive** — A reading taken, not a verdict delivered: an instrument passed to the daughter, or humanity's first line entered into a log that outlasts every reader — the contamination left standing as data, not decreed.

**Forbidden**
- Makers as gardeners who engineered the warming to reclaim or replant Earth (that is Nº 03); any depopulation-as-farming reveal. They keep instruments; they do not want the planet.
- Climate-apocalypse as sermon: drowned-landmark montage, "we did this to ourselves," disaster-porn. Warming is lived as fuel, photoperiod, survey logistics.
- The observatory as temple — glow, hum, runes, a mystical chamber. Wonder arrives only through correctly-read instruments.
- First contact as conversation: a translatable envoy, a negotiation. Contact stays procedural, forensic, one-sided.
- Victory by sabotage or courtroom speech; also the ice-rebuilds-to-seal-them-in beat (that is Nº 04).

**Levers**
- (a) *Structure:* promote Nkiru to a second POV, the descent braided against the first-native's coming-of-age; or give the thread to the settlement authority demanding the dig stop.
- (b) *Darker/warmer:* darker — the responders reset the site, the fever never breaks, the book turns elegy; warmer — humanity is provisionally logged "worth keeping."
- (c) *Scope:* compress to the single season and the descent alone, responders never on-page; or expand to the fjord-continent's politics of silencing the alarm.
