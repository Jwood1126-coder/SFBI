# Nº 46 — The Filament · FAR (Tier B)

1. **Secretly about** — the dread and consolation of being *processed*: a people who trusted the ground of physics itself must live through it softening, learn they were never the disaster's subject but its material, and decide whether being made fit to pass a threshold is deliverance or the quiet erasure of whoever it was that arrived.

2. **Sibling rule** — Nº 48 *Caretaker Star*. Both are physicist-led procedurals where mapping a real astrophysical system reveals ancient engineered regulation of our cosmic environment on a decades clock. Enforce the maker-question split: *Caretaker Star* is a maintenance-failure rescue that reaches *toward* the makers to contact or repair; here the makers are absent and irrelevant, the installation works exactly as designed, nothing can be fixed, and the only act is endurance. The instant anyone sets out to find, hail, or mend the makers — or the drift reads as malfunction rather than intended treatment — this becomes *Caretaker Star*.

3. **The two questions** — *Outward:* how does a solar-system civilization keep power, medicine, timekeeping, and metabolism running while the decay constants and couplings everything is calibrated to slide — parts per million, then per thousand — across a ninety-year transit no delta-v can steer out of? *Inward:* what must Rosa become to stop *correcting* the drift and accept it as a treatment being administered — to let the derangement finish making her people into something that passes?

4. **Dyad** — *Equation:* the drift curve — α and the weak coupling sliding as a function of local dark-matter density, a broad profile peaking mid-transit; every constant-dependent system (reactor reactivity, isotope half-lives, clock ratios, enzyme kinetics) has a tolerance band and a dated day it exits it — a schedule of failures indexed to position on the filament. *Mirror:* Rosa and her daughter Solveig, born into the transit, whose neurochemistry is native to the drifting physics — well only *inside* the derangement Rosa spends her life resisting. The child is what the transit is for; the mother is what carried it to the threshold.

5. **Shape** — Single close third, Rosa Lindqvist, past tense; the airtight POV is the discipline — she covers the entry decades into deep transit and does not reach the far side. Chapters headed by a named constant against its drifting measured value and a transit-day stamp (λ(⁶⁰Co), day 4,110: 0.9987 nominal); parts by depth into the density profile — *approach, entry, deep transit, the peak*. ~130,000 words, ~36 chapters.

6. **The novum's price**
   - *Flesh:* radiotherapy dosimetry and the Mo-99/Tc-99m and I-131 supply fail as decay rates drift — a therapy regime detuning under a patient on a fixed schedule; nerve and enzyme wobble surface as tremor, arrhythmia, lost cognition in real bodies.
   - *Mass / money:* reactors run hot and must be de-rated to stay subcritical (banked energy poverty); every clock, dating instrument, and dose calculator is recalibrated or scrapped, and the bill for permanent re-engineering is a civilization's whole budget.
   - *Time / relationship:* radiocarbon and radiometric geochronology break — the shared clock to the past severed — and the years Rosa spends holding the old physics are years not spent with the daughter only ever well in the new.

7. **Science memo pointers** — Dark matter / large-scale structure: local density (~0.3–0.4 GeV/cm³, Standard Halo Model), the cosmic web and modest filament overdensities, WIMP/axion candidates, survey mapping (DESI/Euclid, DisPerSE). The reality problem to resolve honestly: real DM is collisionless, so state the *single* invented coupling precisely and derive the rest rigorously. Varying constants: α and mass ratio μ; the Oklo reactor bound over 2 Gyr; the quasar-absorption "dipole" controversy; optical clock ratios (Sr, Yb, Al⁺) bounding α̇/α to ~1e-18/yr — the load-bearing *detection* instrument, drift read off diverging clock ratios and isotope geochronology. Decay sensitivity: Gamow-factor tunneling making alpha half-lives exquisitely sensitive to small coupling changes (the ppm→ppt amplification), weak coupling G_F, beta decay; the Jenkins–Fischbach controversy as touchstone. Casualties: the Cs-hyperfine SI second and GNSS timing, reactor kinetics (delayed-neutron fraction β, reactivity), medical isotopes and dosimetry, radiocarbon/K-Ar/U-Pb. Biochemistry: enzyme tunneling, ion-channel and nerve-conduction kinetics, electrostatic tuning of protein folding — the path from EM-coupling shift to nerve-and-enzyme wobble. Frame offstage: anthropic fine-tuning, Rees's six numbers. Exclude: clock aging, magnetic and gravitational-redshift systematics — the drift is smooth and position-indexed, never random.

8. **Opening image candidate** — At shift start Rosa set the strontium clock ticking against the ytterbium and copied out the ratio the two were not supposed to disagree on, now wrong in the eleventh place and, for the fourth month running, wrong the same way.

9. **Ending directive** — An act of record or handoff at the density peak: a last line in the drift ledger, or the back-computed trajectory laid before someone who will outlive the transit — the far side and its screening left in projection, never shown. Close on the number or the gesture; not on arrival, a maker, or a realization.

10. **Forbidden**
    - The makers shown, contacted, or "explained" by a voice; the artificiality stays inferred from gradients, never confirmed by a speaker.
    - The drift as random chaos or physics dissolving into magic; it is smooth and schedulable, and the horror is its regularity — no reality-melting set pieces.
    - The comfort reveal played warm — no "the universe was caring for us all along." The treatment is processing; being improved without consent is not being loved.
    - The evacuation/outrun plot; there is no ark and no escape delta-v (that is Nº 47's book) — only endurance and recalibration.
    - The lone genius who *solves* it: competence buys time and understanding, never a cure, and acceptance arrives as procedure, never epiphany.

11. **Levers** — (a) Multi-generational relay — Rosa diagnoses, Solveig grown reaches the far side and its screening — to actually cross the ninety years, at the cost of the single POV; or dual POV, Rosa's physics against a neurologist charting the drift in bodies. (b) Darker: Earth *fails* the screening, or minds degrade faster than culture adapts; warmer: chamber-scale, the whole transit refracted through one household's recalibration. (c) Compress to the peak-crossing years in one lab (near-real-time); or widen to the political economy of governing ninety continuous years of re-engineering.
