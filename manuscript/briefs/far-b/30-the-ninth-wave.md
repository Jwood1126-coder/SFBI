# Nº 30 — The Ninth Wave · FAR (Tier B)

**Secretly about** — bequeathing an unfinished emergency: keeping a watch no single life can complete, and raising children fit to inherit the danger rather than the solution.

**Sibling rule** — Nº47 *The Ark of Zero*; both are three-generation cosmic deadlines. Enforce the split: 47 flees an unsurvivable, secretly-steered wall — 30 stays and endures a mindless rain, so no exodus and the Fetch never acquires intent or a shaper behind it.

**The two questions** — *Outward:* how do people keep living in an inner system that stays a shooting gallery for a thousand years — survey, shelter, intercept, feed themselves under a falling sky? *Inward:* what must a family become to be good at endurance with no ending to earn?

**Dyad** — *Equation:* the Fetch's ephemeris and the comet-flux curve it drives — impacts per century, sagging toward the spike at the second, closer pass; the budget a civilization is solved against. *Mirror:* a parent drilling a child on a watch neither will see closed — the inherited Longwatch seat.

**Shape** — Three POV, one per part, third-person limited, past tense: Milena, Reza, and Amadou Calder, successive holders of the Longwatch seat — a hereditary office, the name kept because the name *is* the post. Parts I *Detection* / II *The Bombardment Century* / III *The Herders*, each headed by the Fetch's updated ephemeris and flux; chapters headed by Fall Register entries — designation, radiant, predicted fall against actual. ~150,000 words, ~45 chapters.

**The novum's price**
- Milena burns her career proving the Fetch and dies disbelieved; the shelters are poured over her grave — the vindication never reaches the one who earned it.
- A century of watch eats the surplus — mass, money, labor into batteries and shelters; settlements live at subsistence, a generation that never sees an unhurried sky.
- Reza's shelter holds but the dust-winter takes a child or the herd; Amadou's second-pass deflection is bought with the *Godwit* and her crew — flesh and a bond, for a diversion no one downstream will know was close.

**Science memo pointers** — Detecting a dark ~5–10 M☉ black hole near 1200 AU: ephemeris residuals in outer-body and spacecraft ranging (the Planet Nine method, far heavier) and astrometric microlensing (cf. OGLE-2011-BLG-0462), Gaia-class astrometry. Comet dynamics: Oort 1950, the Hills inner cloud, the Heisler–Tremaine tide, the Öpik impulse for a stellar passage — and the crux: infall times for comets kicked at ~1200 AU run 10³–10⁵ yr (period ~ a^1.5), so the compressed "millennium of bombardment" is the book's one license — locate the injected population, defend the leading edge. Impacts: nuclei 1–50 km at 50–70 km/s, Chicxulub to Tunguska, impact winter, dust loading, Shoemaker–Levy 9 for tidal breakup. Defense: kinetic impactors (DART/Hera), gravity tractors, delta-v and warning-time budgets, long-period comets the worst case, nuclear standoff the loaded option. Habitats: closed-loop CO₂ and algal/aquaculture food in deep and sub-seafloor shelters, fission or geothermal power through multi-year dark (never solar). Cadence: Rubin/LSST-class NEO surveys and Sentry risk tables — the "batteries."

**Opening image candidate** — Milena Calder subtracted the planets from the night's astrometry one by one, the way she had for nine nights, and again the residual would not go to zero.

**Ending directive** — An act of continuation, not victory: an object of the watch handed on — the log left open to its next blank line, the seat filled — the second pass survived or not, the register still kept. No last comet, no closing of the sky.

**Forbidden** — (1) The Fetch as a lurking, malevolent presence "coming for us"; it passed, and has no will. (2) The last-second deflection that saves the world — the win is maintenance, not an Armageddon miracle. (3) Any hand steering the comets or the hole (that is 47's book). (4) Softened science: hand-waved infall timescale, comets as fast asteroids, solar power under a dust winter. (5) The falling-sky culture as superstitious decline or mockery — its religion, language, and craft are competent and dignified.

**Levers** — (a) POV: collapse to one long-lived watcher (life-extension the sole novum swap) who spans all three generations, breadth traded for intimacy. (b) Darker: the shelters do *not* mostly hold — Part II becomes attrition and a settlement lost, a study of managed retreat. (c) Scope: contract to Milena's detection-and-disbelief as a standalone astronomy-politics novel, the millennium only implied.
