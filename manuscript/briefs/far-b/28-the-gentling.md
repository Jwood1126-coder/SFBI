# Nº 28 — The Gentling · FAR (Tier B)

**Secretly about** — the grief of the un-consoled: whether discontent is a defect or the last ungentled thing in us, and what it costs a daughter to keep wanting in a world that has forgiven itself everything.

**Sibling rule** — Nº 27 *Fruiting Bodies*. Both land the same wound — humanity was cultivated, a harvest is scheduled — but Fruiting Bodies is a natural-history decipherment whose intelligence is an external network the protagonist reads. The Gentling keeps its change *inside human temperament* and its stage *inside human society* — the schism between gentled majority and immune Stubborn. If Rhea decodes an alien system's signals instead of negotiating a lovingly obstructive human world, the two have merged.

**The two questions** — Outward: how does a shrinking two-percent minority keep a civilization's machines running and drive an expedition across a world that tenderly obstructs it, before the demographic curve finishes them? Inward: what must Rhea refuse — the peace, the cup, the comfort — to stay a wolf worth having when the collectors arrive?

**Dyad** — Equation: the Stubborn are ~2% and falling — attrition, intermarriage into the communes, sub-replacement fertility — a decay curve racing a fixed inheritance of fuel and un-manufacturable parts against the collectors' schedule. Mirror: Rhea and Odile, the partner she could rejoin by taking the Communion — the one door out of the loneliness, and the one she must not walk through.

**Shape** — Close third, single POV (Rhea; the gentled stay opaque, seen only from outside — that opacity is the loneliness). Past tense. Chapters headed by the epidemiological back-trace: dated nodes running from now toward patient zero and the impact site, carrier fraction printed at each head, falling as the years reverse. ~115,000 words; ~36 chapters.

**The novum's price**
- Flesh: partial immunity is real; a trusted companion — or a Stubborn child — with incomplete resistance converts from a shared source mid-expedition, lost while still sitting there, still loving them. The sealed cup is a wall between the Stubborn and everyone they touch.
- Mass/money: the gentled build nothing; the expedition rides cannibalized pre-Communion machinery and a fuel inheritance that only shrinks — every breakdown permanent, every kilometer capital never earned back.
- Time: the years Rhea spends tracing the origin are years the enclave counted on her hands and her body; the curve does not pause, and the search spends the margin it means to save.

**Science memo pointers** — Microbiota-gut-brain axis: enteric nervous system, vagus signaling, tryptophan/serotonin (~90–95% enteric), short-chain fatty acids; behavioral manipulators (*Toxoplasma gondii*/dopamine, *Ophiocordyceps*, *Wolbachia*). Domestication syndrome is the spine — Belyaev's silver-fox selection for tameness, the neural-crest-cell hypothesis (Wilkins/Wrangham/Fitch 2014), depressed fear/aggression/novelty-seeking, human self-domestication. Epidemiology: waterborne fecal-oral spread, Snow's Broad Street pump as back-trace method, R0 and herd-immunity threshold, seroprevalence surveys, molecular-clock phylogenetics to an index case; model the immune 2% on CCR5-Δ32. Water: chlorine-resistant cyst stages (*Cryptosporidium*, *Giardia*) that force distillation. Demography: TFR, replacement 2.1, population momentum, minimum viable / effective population size, drift in small isolates. Xenobiology at the origin: amino-acid chirality, anomalous codon usage — the signature the Communion is not Earth's biochemistry. Hold the collectors to a technosignature and a schedule; never render them.

**Opening image candidate** — Rhea distilled the day's water in a still she tended alone, because her mother had stopped minding open cups the summer Rhea turned nine.

**Ending directive** — End on an act that decides whether the wolves stay wolves — a cup lifted and set down untouched, or a thing built no gentled hand would build — the collectors' schedule still only a number, never a shape. Do not show the collection; do not resolve whether wildness is salvation or spite.

**Forbidden**
- The gentled as pod-people or a dystopia to overthrow; their peace is genuine, good, enviable — the horror is that it is not evil.
- Rhea's discontent flattered as the "last free human" triumph; her hunger must read as affliction as often as virtue.
- The Communion's purpose delivered by a villain's monologue or a lab tape; it is inferred from biochemistry and epidemiology or not at all.
- A cure, a mass waking-up, humanity's edge restored — reversal is not the win and is banned as an ending.
- The collectors shown — fleet, face, beast; they stay a protocol and a date to the last page.

**Levers** — (a) Add a gentled second POV — a caretaker whose contented interior we enter — to make the peace unbearable from inside; or hold strict single-Rhea to keep the gentled opaque. (b) Darker: the Communion is wholly benign and the collectors the only monsters, so the Stubborn's leverage condemns the gentled to a fought-over herd; warmer: the collection is something the gentled were prepared to *survive*, and Rhea's wildness is the error. (c) Scope: a chamber piece in one failing enclave, origin traced through records and one informant; or the full continental road.

**Registry** — **Rhea Ostrom**, protagonist, one of the Stubborn (immune, ambitious). **Odile**, her gentled former partner, the mirror. **Holdfast**, her failing Stubborn enclave. The cannibalized overland hauler, **Dray**. Coined terms (three, all spent): **the Communion** (symbiont), **the Stubborn** (immune ~2%), **the Gentling** (the process).
