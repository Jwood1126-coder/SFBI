# Nº 19 — Inclusion · FAR (Tier C)

**0. The repair** — As written this is Nº31 *Not a Place of Honor* moved to the belt: a deep-time warning in durable geology, a sealed cell whose occupant *wakes*, translation-as-descent, rivals forcing the seal. The prisoner who "begs whoever found it to leave the lights off" is the exact shown-occupant/waking-god move the Tier A cards (11, 14, 26, 31) forbid; the crystal-inclusion message also duplicates Nº39 *Lagerstätte*'s beacon. One change fixes it: **nothing sleeps in the geode.** The entity is four billion years gone and wrote itself into inert mineral order precisely to *stop being itself*. The confession is self-erasure, not a cell; the only hazard is reconstitution — assaying, warming, and refining the walls reassembles the template into reactive matter. Now reading is mining and saving the world is going broke, and the book owns an ending no sibling can: she must destroy the richest strike in history to keep the sentence unfinished.

**1. Secretly about** — The loneliest form of integrity: keeping faith with a stranger you will never meet and can never prove, at a price no one will ever see or repay.

**2. Sibling rule** — Nº31 *Not a Place of Honor*. Enforce: 31 ends at an uncrossed threshold with a discrete occupant behind a seal; 19 has no door and no thing — the hazard is diffuse in the ore, and the climax is the destruction of value, not restraint at a wall.

**3. The two questions** — Outward: how does a solo prospector, months from rescue and low on reaction mass, keep the claim-jumpers she summoned from stripping a rock she cannot defend, and outlast her own consumables doing it? Inward: what must a woman who converts everything to mass and price become, to vaporize the only fortune she will ever be offered because a dead stranger asked her to?

**4. Dyad** — Equation: completion fraction vs. mining rate vs. consumables — the template reconstitutes past a read-threshold her assay has already crossed and the jumpers' extraction will finish, so she must un-read faster than they mine, against a hard clock of O2-days and delta-v. Mirror: the one-way intimacy between Sabine and the confessor — across four billion years she comes to know a stranger better than anyone alive knows her, and must betray her whole future to keep that stranger's one request.

**5. Shape** — Single POV, Sabine Vex, close third, past tense. Chapters headed by her prospecting ledger — a fixed gauge stack (reaction-mass kg, consumables-days, nearest rival's ETA, assay-depth cm, translation-complete %), the far voice's checklist with one line wrong. The decoded confession accretes in short italic interchapters, so the reader assembles the sentence as the ore is read. ~110,000 words, ~33 chapters, three parts.

**6. The novum's price**
- Mass and flesh: every stratum she reads spends reaction mass she needs to leave 21771 and adds EVA dose she cannot undo — certainty bought in kilograms and rads, and to be *sure* the rock is dangerous she must nearly complete the danger.
- Money and dynasty: the walls are salted with platinum-group and rare-earth metals, an itemized generational fortune. The strike she already broadcast to secure it is what dooms her; to keep the pattern incomplete she must vaporize the assay she crossed four hundred million kilometers to sell.
- A person: the broker-partner she promised to bring home rich, and every future that money bought — spent in one act no one will witness or believe.

**7. Science memo pointers** — Prospecting: reflectance spectroscopy and asteroid taxonomy (M/S/C-types), Tsiolkovsky and delta-v budgets, low-thrust vs. Hohmann transfers, main-belt one-way light-time, power at 2–3 AU (RTG vs. solar); verify or replace the catalog number 21771 against the real minor-planet database. Assay hardware: XRF, LIBS, Raman, mass and gamma/neutron spectrometry, micro-CT. Mineralogy for "chemistry as text": isotopic ratios, trace-element zoning, crystallographic ordering, mineral-replacement textures, solid/fluid inclusions. Economics/law: PGE and REE grades, Outer Space Treaty and the 2015 Space Act resource vacuum. The one implausibility, guard-railed with real chemistry: prion templating; autocatalytic crystallization (polymorph seeding, "disappearing polymorphs," Ostwald ripening); information theory (Shannon capacity, erasure/error-correcting codes — how much intact signal reconstructs a message). Deep time: solar-system age 4.567 Gyr, parent-body differentiation and thermal metamorphism, radiometric dating; the sphere's geometric perfection is the tell no natural process makes. Solo survival: closed-loop ECLSS, CO2/water margins, EVA career dose limits, solar particle events, single-crew confinement psychology (overwinter and submarine analogs).

**8. Opening image candidate** — She had the first core clamped in the assay cradle and the laser already warming when the mineralogy came back wrong — not poor, wrong: elements racked into rows no melt had ever poured.

**9. Ending directive** — A single physical act of un-reading, witnessed by no one and logged in the same ledger that has counted her costs all book — a gauge zeroed, a core voided, a claim withdrawn; no rescue confirmed, no future reader promised. Content open; the shape is subtraction.


**10. Forbidden**
- The waking monster: no scene where the occupant stirs, speaks, or is beheld. Nothing is alive down there; the repair dies if it breathes.
- The confession as ancient-alien monologue — a voice with feelings pleading across the stone. It stays inference from chemistry, never speech.
- The mad-prospector out: cabin-fever hallucination that lets the reader file the find as delusion. Clarity over subtlety — the rock is real and verified.
- The morality play: greed punished by cosmic comeuppance, or a rescue that spares her the cost. She pays in full, and lives.
- "Chemistry as language" hand-waved into magic runes. If it cannot be built from real mineralogy and information theory, cut it.

**11. Levers** — (a) Found-document form: first person, present tense, the assay log as the entire text — the reader decodes only what the instruments give her. (b) Warmer: a light-lagged human interlocutor (broker, ex, child) on a 40-minute delay, so the destroyed fortune has a face begging her to sell. (c) Scope: compress to the last sixty hours from the jumpers' arrival (a taut novella), or widen to the belt's reaction to a strike deliberately unmade.
