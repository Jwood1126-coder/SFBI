# Nº 37 — Cloud Garden · FAR (Tier C)

0. **The repair** — As written the book is a whodunit over a buried secret — who spliced the second genome, which concealed contact event — a mystery-of-fact the clarity law forbids, and it duplicates Nº36 Second Eden beat for beat: engineered-algae terraforming is secretly a garden for an alien, and the human project must choose to complete it or stop it. Two flaws, one change: establish the double bottom as known fact by chapter one and delete the stop option. The colony is fifty kilometres up and cannot sterilize a planet; the garden finishes on schedule regardless. The only live question — present from the first page, mechanical, unhidden — is whether Ferraz can engineer the human cultivar a permitted niche inside a garden completing for its client, or whether one seed always strangles the other. Reveal-thriller becomes husbandry under an arriving partner.

1. **Secretly about** — Whether a people can build a life inside something that was never built for them — learning to want a future in which you are a tenant, not an heir, and call that enough.

2. **Sibling rule** — Nº36 Second Eden. Both are algae-terraforming-as-secret-alien-garden. Enforce the tense: Second Eden exhumes a past terraforming and a buried thing waking to be contained (backward, geological, horror of resurrection); Cloud Garden's client has never been here — it is unborn and inbound, a vehicle on a braking schedule, and the drama is prospective coexistence. The instant something is "waking" or "was buried to trap it," this is Second Eden on Venus.

3. **The two questions** — Outward: how does a floating colony stay lofted and alive in an atmosphere a rival organism is re-chemistrying out from under its envelopes, on the schedule of a client it has never met? Inward: can a woman raised to inherit a world learn to engineer herself a smaller place in someone else's — to be grafted rather than to own?

4. **Dyad** — Equation: competitive exclusion in the cloud chemostat — the human cultivar and the garden draw on one limiting feedstock, and R* theory says one goes extinct unless Ferraz opens a second limiting axis before the client's vehicle brakes into orbit on a computable date. Mirror: Ferraz's infant, the first child born on Nimbus, whose inheritable band the garden is quietly reconditioning shut.

5. **Shape** — Single close third, Lucia Ferraz, present tense. Device: chapters headed by a paired reading — the garden's developmental stage (an embryology-atlas staging of the alien program's expression) against the day's atmospheric assay (acid molarity, altitude of neutral buoyancy, days-to-insertion) — the gestation clock ticking under the human scenes. ~115,000 words, ~33 chapters.

6. **The novum's price**
- Flesh/consumables: assaying the maturing structures means EVA into the growing cloud forms in an acid-rated suit; every close sample spends suit integrity, sequencing reagents lofted up the gravity well, and skin.
- Habitat/mass: the garden cracks the same sulfuric acid and CO₂ the aerostats' buoyancy and envelopes are engineered around; as it conditions the deck the habitable band narrows and drifts, and holding altitude costs ballast, lift-gas, and finally a whole aerostat cut loose.
- Relationship: the graft that buys the human cultivar a niche is paid for by conceding the garden's primacy — a surrender her partner, whose family and stake ride on the human schedule, reads as treason.

7. **Science memo pointers** — Venus aerostat engineering (NASA HAVOC lineage): breathable air as lifting gas in a 96.5% CO₂ atmosphere, the ~50–55 km habitable band (~0.5–1 bar, 0–50 °C), acid-resistant envelopes (FEP/PTFE, PBO). Venus atmospheric chemistry: 75–98% H₂SO₄ cloud droplets, SO₂, the unknown UV absorber, Mode 1/2/3 particles; the phosphine (Greaves 2020) and water-activity (Hallsworth 2021) controversies at the live-debate level, never the pop version. Cloud astrobiology: acidophile and water-activity limits, the neutralization/ammonia hypothesis (Seager). Bioinformatics: flagging a horizontal insertion by codon-usage and GC anomaly, ORF annotation, HMMs, developmental gene-regulatory networks and morphogenesis (Turing patterns, the French-flag model). Population biology (the spine): Tilman R* resource-ratio theory, chemostat competitive exclusion, Monod kinetics — "one seed strangles the other" is the competitive-exclusion principle, and the graft is niche differentiation on a second limiting nutrient. Orbital mechanics: Venus aerocapture/aerobraking, the arrival vehicle's delta-v and a datable insertion. Synthetic biology of the graft: orthogonal codes, engineered regulatory circuits, gene-drive containment failure modes.

8. **Opening image candidate** — Ferraz draws the night's sample from Nimbus's harvester, rinses the acid off the vial, and racks it to read how far down the double bottom the garden ran while the aerostat slept.

9. **Ending directive** — End on an act of her trade — a construct written into the cloud deck, a graft released or withheld — that commits the shared garden to one architecture; the garden's answer and the client's arrival left off the page. An object or an act, never a realization.

10. **Forbidden**
- The whodunit: the sabotage hunt, the concealed contact event as late reveal, the Consortium conspiracy thriller — all deleted by the repair.
- The wise womb: the clouds as loving, sentient mother; the garden metabolizes and selects, it does not cherish.
- First contact as parley: the client rendered as an envoy who lands and talks; it stays biological, inbound, and unmet.
- Venus-cathedral purple: awe by adjective at the luminous alien forms — wonder arrives only through an instrument read correctly.
- Graft as rapture or triumph: humans saving or merging with the garden, a clean two-species harmony, or a noble human die-off; the end is terms, not transcendence or martyrdom.

11. **Levers** — (a) POV/structure: interchapters decoding the garden's own expression logs as data, or a second POV in the partner on the Consortium schedule. (b) Darker/warmer — darker: the two programs share one regulatory locus (one promoter, two clients), so coexistence is chemically false and the choice is which the garden aborts; warmer: the client's program carries a commensal, dormant branch Ferraz can express, making humans a permitted epiphyte. (c) Scope: contract to a single aerostat and the arrival window (chamber piece), or widen to the whole Cirrus fleet and rival powers racing to claim or weaponize the garden.
