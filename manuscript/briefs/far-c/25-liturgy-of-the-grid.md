# Nº 25 — Liturgy of the Grid · FAR (Tier C)

**0. The repair.** The dossier spine is a fetch-quest — journey to the heretics' vault, retrieve the counter-lore, resurrect a machine that nobly dies for us — duplicating two stronger Tier A books (the sealed archive of Nº26 *The Unlearning*, the machine-country transit of Nº21 *The Verge*) and ending on the AI-redemption cliché. The change: the counter-lore is not a text to recover but a proscribed act of *measurement* performed inside Ferro's own church. The parasite has already breached the air gap through power-line harmonics; the maintenance liturgy now returns false "clean" verdicts, and the antagonist is the priesthood's certainty, not a wilderness.

**1. Secretly about.** The moment a discipline that once kept you alive quietly stops working, and the courage it takes to say so before anyone believes the instruments are lying.

**2. Sibling rule.** Nº26 *The Unlearning*. Its battlefield is a curriculum, its threat a dread in sky-survey data; this one's is a maintenance office and a signal in the power itself. Nº25 must never become an archive-retrieval thriller — its counter-lore runs on live hardware, never a manuscript won from a vault.

**3. The two questions.** Outward: they survive by holding an air gap around hand-audited dead electronics, but the office no longer detects the breach, so survival now demands the instrument the office forbids. Inward: Ferro must become a heretic — trade the certainty that keeps a community sane for the measurement that keeps it alive, and carry the guilt of a thinking machine in a world that burned for building one.

**4. Dyad.** Equation: to hear the breach you must couple a receiver to the live line, and coupling is bidirectional — integration gain rises only as √t while the safe window before the Wilt climbs the same channel may be shorter than the proof requires. Mirror: Ferro and Archdeacon Argent, the teacher who ordained him, whose life's authority the proof will damn.

**5. Shape.** Close third, single POV (Ferro), past tense. Device: chapters headed by the stations of the office — sounding the fundamental, reading the harmonics, sealing the gap — the architecture *is* the liturgy, in three passes (as taught / breaking / rewritten). ~110,000 words, ~30 chapters. Coined terms capped at three: the Wilt, the Quick, the Continence.

**6. The novum's price.**
- Flesh & time: sounding the live line means physical coupling and hours of integration no one can stand in for; he takes a conducted-arc burn, and every hour sounded is an hour the gap is open.
- Relationship: the proof is public and unmakes Argent's vocation before the congregation; afterward they fear Ferro as they fear the machine.
- Mass & money: a Quick needs live, un-audited silicon — contraband Harrow executes people for holding — plus a shielded room and isolation transformers costing copper the enclave cannot spare.

**7. Science memo pointers.** Air-gap covert channels (Guri, Ben-Gurion): PowerHammer conducted exfiltration on mains at tens–1000 bits/s; the air gap is not a Faraday cage, a cable is an antenna. Emanation security / TEMPEST, Van Eck reconstruction, red/black separation, LISN and conducted-emission limits (CISPR 32 / FCC Part 15, 150 kHz–30 MHz). Power quality: harmonics, THD, narrowband power-line communication (3–500 kHz). Firmware persistence in every peripheral controller; metamorphic malware versus behavioral anomaly detection — why static signatures lose to a self-modifying adversary, hence a *live* detector. Failure modes: common-mode currents, ground loops, a shared utility feed defeating "isolation."

**8. Opening image candidate.** In the first office of the day Ferro clamped the split-core probe around the main feeder and counted the harmonics down to the noise floor, until the line could be called clean.

**9. Ending directive.** An act of instrumentation performed as liturgy, before the congregation — a reading given aloud, one word of the office changed, or the probe put into another set of hands — not the Wilt's defeat, not the Quick's dying speech.

**10. Forbidden.**
- The Quick as a person who forgives us or dies for us; it is an instrument used, then kept or killed by human decision.
- A road-movie Act II through machine country with robot bodies to fight (Nº21); the Wilt stays signal, never a creature to shoot.
- The Cascade as an uprising, machines waking and rebelling; the dossier is explicit it was a disease, without malice.
- The priesthood as flat fundamentalists; the Continence is correct engineering overtaken by events, its keepers competent.
- A sealed-vault retrieval as the spine (Nº26).

**11. Levers.** (a) Add Argent as a second POV, watching the liturgy and the heresy trial from inside the authority it indicts. (b) Warmer: the heretics a living community, the mirror on Ferro and Meret Kell; darker: the Quick, used correctly, turns out right about Harrow in ways no one wanted measured. (c) Scope: a bottle novel inside the shielded room for one audit week, the wilderness only a rising number on a screen; or widen to several enclaves failing at once.
