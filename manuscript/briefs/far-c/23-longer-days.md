# Nº 23 — Longer Days · FAR (Tier C)

0. **The repair** — The dossier's "filament of dark mass threaded through the core" is unphysical (dark matter is collisionless; it cannot torque a spin) and a lesser clone of Nº 46 *The Filament*. Cut dark matter. The theft is a legible tidal coupling: an engineered mass, *the Escapement*, in resonant orbit beyond the Moon, phasing Earth's tidal bulge for maximum braking torque and exporting the spin off-system a million times faster than the Moon does. Physics stays textbook; one named conserved quantity is stolen. This clears Nº 46 and lands the novum on Earth's rotation, where the book is strong.

1. **Secretly about** — inheriting a debt you never signed: living out ruined days to service a bargain the dead struck in our name, and deciding whether a species can consent to a sacrifice made wholly for the ones who come after.

2. **Sibling rule** — Nº 22 *Drawdown*: both are a scientist's forensic hunt for a hidden agent draining a planetary quantity, an ancient prior claimant behind it. Enforce native-reclamation vs contracted-toll — *Drawdown*'s agent takes a stolen home back; the Escapement is an off-world creditor collecting a toll our proxies contracted, and *protecting* us from a third thing. If the harvester ever wants Earth for itself, this is *Drawdown*.

3. **The two questions** — *Outward:* how do people feed and shelter themselves as the day stretches past thirty then forty hours — bulge relaxing, photoperiod agriculture failing, weather reorganizing around a terminator no longer moving at human pace? *Inward:* what must Reyes become to pay a toll he never agreed to, knowing payment dooms his daughter's world and default may be worse.

4. **Dyad** — *Equation:* the angular-momentum ledger — a braking torque against a blank source column, the length-of-day curve climbing on schedule, the day the reservoir reads full. *Mirror:* Ezequiel and Beatriz, a marriage the widening day prises apart — he chases the terminator, she burrows to a sealed 24-hour clock; two people who can no longer share a day, raising Suri in shifts across the dawn line.

5. **Shape** — Dual close third (Reyes, the theft; Beatriz, the crop-failure front), past tense; chapters headed by the current day-length and LOD residual (*day 04:00 · UT1−UTC +6h 12m, climbing*). ~130,000 words, ~34 chapters; parts by the day's length — the 26-, 30-, 40-hour day.

6. **The novum's price**
   - *Flesh / time:* human entrainment ceilings near 24 h — a 40-hour day is non-entrainable; bodies decouple, Suri's clock breaks, mortality climbs.
   - *Money / mass:* as spin slows the equatorial bulge relaxes — sea level falls at the equator, rises poleward; ports built for a fast planet drown, and tidal heating and slack Coriolis wreck the harvest.
   - *Relationship:* the chaser/burrower split severs the marriage; the family survives only in the handoff at the terminator.

7. **Science memo pointers** — Earth-rotation geodesy: length-of-day and UT1−UTC, IERS/IVS products, VLBI, ring-laser gyroscopes (Wettzell "G"), GNSS, lunar laser ranging, superconducting gravimeters; the ~1 ms LOD variations and the angular-momentum budget (atmospheric AM, core-mantle coupling, the Moon's secular braking at +2.3 ms/century) — detection is a residual torque that won't close the ledger. Tidal mechanics: Love number k₂, tidal Q, phase lag, the export path. Consequences to derive, not montage: the diurnal energy balance of a 30–40 h day (runaway afternoon, deep radiative night); rotational-flattening relaxation and its sea-level and gravity signature; Coriolis weakening; tidal dissipation as geothermal load. Chronobiology: photoperiodism (phytochrome, CO/FT flowering), the suprachiasmatic nucleus, non-24-h desync. Hard constraint: angular momentum and energy must go somewhere — most exported, or the mantle melts.

8. **Opening image candidate** — Reyes ran the day's angular-momentum ledger the way he ran it every shift — atmosphere, ocean, core, the Moon's old steady bite — and for the ninth week it closed long, a braking torque standing against a source column he had left, and would have to leave, blank.

9. **Ending directive** — An act at an instrument, not a speech: a value keyed into a field, a coupling left engaged or broken, the day's length logged once more — the toll's purpose named only in projection, and which way Reyes moved the world left as the last recorded number, not a feeling.

10. **Forbidden**
    - Dark matter or the "filament" image restored — the coupling is tidal, physics untouched; that book is Nº 46.
    - The Escapement or its makers shown or voiced; the theft stays inferred from ledger and orbit — infrastructure, never speech.
    - The "we were cared for all along" warm reveal (also Nº 48's ripening); protection without consent is not love.
    - Day-length as generic climate montage; the horror is the arithmetic — diurnal range, bulge, photoperiod.
    - The lone-genius reversal: Reyes cannot re-spin a planet; competence buys a choice, never a cure.

11. **Levers** — (a) Collapse to single close third on Reyes, or add a third POV at the Escapement's characterization probe — at the cost of the domestic intimacy. (b) Darker: humanity defaults, breaks the coupling, and the last act turns outward to what the toll held back; warmer: chamber-scale, one household learning a 40-hour day, physics offstage. (c) Compress to the detection year, or widen across decades to a 100-hour day and Suri grown, inheriting the choice.
