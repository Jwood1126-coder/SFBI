# Nº 34 — Grazer · FAR (Tier C)

**0. The repair** — The dossier's Act III is Nº16 Bait in a lower orbit: a cosmic fisherman, livestock, a "negotiation over the harvest." Bait (Tier A) owns the angler-fishes-for-civilizations thesis and does it at interstellar scale, so Grazer as written is the lesser retread. Repair: delete the intelligence. There is no fisherman. The inward-pointing "bait line" is a real gradient with no author — a tissue-thin, extreme area-to-mass organism (a near-zero ballistic coefficient) whose orbit decays *downward* through the debris shells under atmospheric drag, far faster than the dense metal it eats, on a schedule fixed by its own drag and the solar-cycle density of the thermosphere; humanity built the richest metal reef in the system by accident, and the bloom is descending straight through it. The engine becomes ecological indifference, not predation: nothing is coming, and there is no one to bargain with. One deletion de-collides the book and hands it a harder thesis.

**1. Secretly about** — A fixer's reckoning with the one catastrophe she cannot repair, and her discovery that accurate witness, not rescue, is the last competent act.

**2. Sibling rule** — Nº16 Bait. Bait is a crewed voyage toward a lure that wants us; Grazer stays in Earth orbit and its antagonist is an animal with no interest in us at all. Enforce: never disclose a will behind the bloom, or this is Bait in orbit.

**3. The two questions** — Outward: how does one engineer stay alive on a structure being metabolized out from under her, and which orbits can a doomed civilization triage before the commons digests itself? Inward: can a woman whose whole identity is repair become a witness — trade the reflex to fix for the discipline to record a thing she cannot stop?

**4. Dyad** — Equation: grazer biomass doubling (~9-day) against the fixed metal budget of low orbit, atmospheric drag walking the bloom-front *down* through the shells — every high-drag membrane decaying on a schedule computable from its ballistic coefficient and thermospheric density — so every catalogued object has a datable hour of ingestion before the front reaches its altitude, including Gleaner's. Mirror: the last comsat on that schedule is the one relaying her downlink to Ify; the death of the sky is also the closing of the only channel between them.

**5. Shape** — Single POV (Adaeze), close third, present tense. Structural device: each chapter headed by a catalog entry being retired — an object subtracted from the debris registry, with its last-observed timestamp; midway the entries become the comms and nav satellites she depends on, and last of all Gleaner's own transponder. ~115,000 words, ~34 chapters.

**6. The novum's price**
- Flesh/consumables: the best data — the dividing event, the gradient assay — demands close EVAs into the grazing zone that spend cumulative rem and irreplaceable life-support margin; the better her witness, the sooner she dies.
- Money/relationship: Verdigris Reclamation writes Gleaner off as a total loss and cuts her stipend and resupply, so she is now unpaid salvage — and she can compute the date the bloom reaches the satellite carrying Ify's downlink.
- Mass: to hold station she must jettison mass, and the only expendable mass is the module holding her stored specimens and logs; she must trade the archive itself — the witness — for the propellant to keep witnessing.

**7. Science memo pointers** — Kessler syndrome and debris dynamics (Space Surveillance Network / 18th SDS catalog, two-line elements, conjunction data messages, closing velocities 7–15 km/s in LEO, the 550–1000 km debris shells); area-to-mass ratio and ballistic coefficient, thermospheric density models and their solar-cycle drivers (F10.7, Ap), and drag-driven orbital decay as the real, computable "gradient" — the low-ballistic-coefficient organism sinks through the shells far faster than the debris it eats, and its descent, not any sunward push, is what dates each ingestion. The memo must say plainly why the obvious culprits are *not* the driver: on a bound orbit solar radiation pressure is a net radially outward (anti-sunward) perturbation, and Poynting–Robertson drag is a heliocentric, micron-scale effect whose timescale is astronomically long at kilometre scale — neither moves a macroscopic body sunward in LEO, where atmospheric drag dominates and decays orbits toward Earth. Vacuum astrobiology: EXPOSE/BIOMEX survival results, Deinococcus radiodurans, lichen and tardigrade tolerance; electromicrobiology and extracellular electron transfer (Geobacter, Shewanella), bioleaching acidophiles (Acidithiobacillus) for metal metabolism — and the hard energetics the memo must solve, the electron acceptor and energy source in vacuum (photolithotrophy). Structure/thermal: kilometre-scale membrane tensile limits, sublimation and outgassing, radiative heat balance across eclipse. Observation hardware: reflectance spectroscopy, planetary radar (Haystack, Goldstone) and LIDAR for debris tracking, mass spectrometry of samples. Life support: CO2 scrubbing, O2 electrolysis, water recovery, consumables and power/thermal budgets; radiation dose in Sv, SPE and GCR, career limits. The cascade beneath: GNSS position-navigation-timing under grid synchronization, finance, and telecom — name the real failure modes.

**8. Opening image candidate** — Adaeze runs the morning conjunction check and finds the catalog three objects lighter than the model allows — each one not decayed but subtracted — and reaches for a field in the log the manual never gave her.

**9. Ending directive** — End on an act of transmission with no confirmed receiver: the final observation committed to a record whose reader may not exist, the channel closing on the frequency it opened on. An object handed forward or a line sent — never a realization.

**10. Forbidden**
- The fisherman: no disclosed intelligence, no farmer, no bargain over a harvest — that is Bait.
- The cavalry: no last-act weapon or burn that saves the commons; the commons dies.
- The organism as malice: no stalking, no "it turned toward her"; its danger is incidental — it is grazing.
- Awe by adjective: no prose-poems to the translucent leviathan; wonder arrives only through an instrument read correctly.
- The castaway breakdown: no hallucinated company, no madness arc; isolation is a working condition, not a descent.

**11. Levers**
- (a) Dual POV — add Ify at the ground station; render the sky's death from beneath (a grid or fishing fleet losing PNT) as counterpoint to Adaeze above.
- (b) Darker/warmer — reveal the bloom as a dispersal or spawning run that will carry Earth's metals and Adaeze's record outward: catastrophe as seeding, warmer without restoring a bargaining mind.
- (c) Scope — compress to a single-platform, three-week consumables chamber piece, or expand to a global mosaic of the falling sky with Adaeze as one thread.
