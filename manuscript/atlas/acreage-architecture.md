# ACREAGE — Book Architecture
### The page a future editor opens first. Companion to BIBLE.md §1; governed by §2 (voice law) and §0. Book Four. v1, 2026-07-08.

---

## What this book is

**Title:** ACREAGE — *stories.* Ten stories in the 2029–2044 near future of THE GRAIN, each set where a public institution — a fishery, a court, a grid, a language, a county, a river — takes one irreversible, man-made blow and must turn it into procedure. It is the public-scale sibling of THE GRAIN: colder, civic, its gauges bureaucratic, its grief collective.

THE GRAIN measured the private interior and found the reading was never the thing. ACREAGE turns to the public body: what is an institution *for* when the fact it must process is a loss it cannot reverse? Its wager — an institution is how a community decides what a fact means, and the ground absorbs whatever procedure cannot hold; the remainder is collective, not private. It must not become THE GRAIN again: not one more provincial expert signing a name to a number over a private grief. Here the gauge belongs to an institution, the blow is structural (a launch contract, a de-energization schedule, a denial algorithm, a tribunal), and the drama is *translation* — a human loss rendered into a filing, a count, a verdict. It parts from Book Three (domestic) and Book Two (cosmic) by staying terrestrial: its facts are answerable, and the story is what people do inside the institutions built to answer them.

## The arc

**Downrange** opens. A Gulf shrimper idles at the exclusion wire, range red, while the constellation heavies loft from a port that took her water — the sky filling as the fishery empties, the buyout letters only ratifying it. The question at its coldest: when the blow is structural and no filing reverses it, is there anything to do but idle at the wire and count the fuel?

**The Last Dark** closes. Maeve pencils the next name three years out in a reservation book, for a certified dark sky she has calculated will not last two. It answers: you keep the book; you host the dark while it lasts — stewardship is the reply the fact will not give. The bright sky is the book's spine — a fishery lost early, the dark itself late. The last word, as in THE GRAIN, belongs to the living keeping faith.

## Movements

Three stages in an institution's metabolizing of a loss. Subjects and registers interleave *within* each part so none becomes a single-register trough (THE GRAIN's Part III error).

- **Part I — THE ASSESSMENT** · the blow arrives and is given a number. **Downrange · Manual Review · Nightbloom · Evidential.**
- **Part II — THE PROCEEDINGS** · the institution metabolizes it — filings, audits, tribunals, and one thing built instead of mourned. **Good Offices · QUORUM (commissioned, below) · Standing · Corroboration.**
- **Part III — THE STEWARDSHIP** · what a person keeps past the procedure's reach. **Comfort Measures · Viability · The Last Dark.**

**Splits (binding).** The three machine-instrument stories — Evidential (a dying-language model), Corroboration (a totalizing-recall Instrument), Good Offices (a translation layer) — never sit adjacent (positions 4, 8, 5); the two bright-sky pieces are the far-apart poles; QUORUM breaks the document-heavy Proceedings at its center.

## Range requirements

**Every one of the ten briefs is 3rd limited, and the roster has no major-key pole — THE GRAIN's two exact hard lessons.**

THE GRAIN diversified POV — a first person, a second person, a documentary, an almost-all-dialogue story — to break the monotone. ACREAGE has document texture (five of ten braid transcripts or filings) but zero first-person voice, zero second person, and a uniformly cold-procedural-elegiac register end to end — the "learns the move and braces" trough the memo named THE GRAIN's chief liability. Formal daring is covered by **Good Offices**' paired-column form (a delegate's words against the layer's rendering), which makes the reader perform the audit.

Larger: no comedy, no eros, no youth wanting something and chasing it — the vitality pole THE GRAIN got last and resequenced around. ACREAGE commissions it from day one — an institution not mourned but *born*.

> **Commission — QUORUM** (provisional). First person, present tense, young organizer (late twenties), ~4,200 words. In a de-energized county, a scrappy campaign wins the charter for a community-owned micro-utility — the world's arc bending up. Register: major key — desire on the page, the comedy of civic process (the quorum that won't form, the meeting to midnight), momentum. **Forbidden:** any death, mourned trade, heirloom worn smooth, grief ledger, loss-of-place elegy. **Ending:** motion or speech opening outward — the vote carried, the switch thrown. The vitality pole and the book's only first person; draft it early.

## Voice delta

BIBLE §2 holds entire. Amendments:

1. **The worksite is bureaucratic.** Rule 10 binds, but the day's task is often procedure — a denial code, a germination count, an audit column, a pleading; trade-lock (rule 1) extends to it.
2. **The remainder is collective** — what a *place* loses that no record holds. The mirror bond scales up: to a place, a sibling on shared ground, a town.
3. **Cold open, warm close; the warmth is stewardship, never sentiment** — an act of keeping, never a stated feeling.
4. **The document must not turn metronome**, as the appositive-gloss dash did in THE GRAIN: no more than two adjacent document-built stories.
5. **The major key is mandatory** (QUORUM), against the procedural-of-loss default.

**Calibration.** The same beat — a water lawyer recording a river's low flow — two ways.

*GRAIN-voice (~70 w):* Maren read the gauge the way her father had taught her, the staff worn pale at the four-foot mark, and wrote the number in the field book. The cottonwoods stood back from the cut where the water used to reach. She capped the pen, squared the book on the truck seat, and drove the section road home in the dark, and did not turn the radio on.

*ACREAGE-voice (~70 w):* The gauge read 4.1 feet. The motion needed a want, so she gave the river one in the only grammar the court accepted: a minimum instantaneous flow, eleven cubic feet per second, priority date 1889. She filed it in triplicate by four. Below the intake the cottonwoods stood back from the cut at the old line, keeping a count the docket had no field to receive.

## Canon posture

**ACREAGE lives in the 2029–2044 GRAIN world**, and is the book where the *civic* canon runs load-bearing.

- **Load-bearing here (reserved):** the **bright sky** — the spine, load-bearing in Downrange and The Last Dark (set at the Rand Basin preserve); **Grid Rationalization** — load-bearing in Comfort Measures, glancing in Standing and Viability; the **bees** (Nightbloom) and **Pareon** (Manual Review, one denied refill) glance only.
- **New ACREAGE canon** (log on first naming, load-bearing once each, dated in-span): the dying-language model (Evidential), the totalizing-recall **Instrument** (Corroboration), the translation **layer** (Good Offices), the automated claims-review system (Manual Review) — civic-scale AI nova; each obeys the function test and the anthropomorphism ban.
- **Off-limits beyond a glance** (load-bearing elsewhere): the Adeyemi Signature (Book Two), the consciousness index and every interior-legibility instrument (THE GRAIN, Book Three), Vestry-class AIs, the Completed Life Act.
- **Year-stamp every story** (~2033 → ~2043); keep the bright sky's hardening legible as motion — the linkage the memo asked for. No character crosses between stories or books.

## Commission order

Anchor the poles and the vitality story first; the fragile machine stories next; the keepers last.

1. **Downrange** and **The Last Dark** — the bright-sky poles; fix the temperature range and the spine's far-apart year-stamps.
2. **QUORUM** — the major key, early; THE GRAIN got its vitality story last and paid for it.
3. **Evidential · Corroboration · Good Offices** — the hardest; each can tip into machine-pathos or theme-on-the-sleeve, and all three must be pulled apart. Draft them cross-aware.
4. **Manual Review** — the coldest; hold the ninety-second discipline; no villain, no redemption.
5. **Nightbloom · Viability · Standing · Comfort Measures** — strong forms, lower risk, last.

**Second look before drafting.** The three machine stories (distinct nova and registers); **Comfort Measures** (the county-as-patient conceit kept from cuteness and eulogy); the four document-braided stories (Standing, Manual Review, Good Offices, Corroboration — braid not converging); and POV — QUORUM first person, one brief pulled off 3rd limited (Standing to document-built, or Evidential to margin-notes-only), one considered for second person.

## Done criteria

A story is done when it obeys §2 and the five amendments, lands its ending directive, and survives read-aloud. The book is done when it clears THE GRAIN's inherited audits:

- **Banned-phrase scan** (§2 rule 8) — mechanical, zero tolerance.
- **Name and prop greps** — the EDIT-NOTES texture class (candy, seed-cracking, scanners, talismans) *plus this book's master-image ration:* the kept record (reservation book, field book, germination log, seed envelopes) saturates the roster — ACREAGE's signature *and* its trap, as the notebook (×9) was THE GRAIN's; cap it, and never two adjacent stories closing on the same keeping gesture.
- **Phrase-frequency n-gram scan** — any construction at 5+ across 5+ stories joins the ration.
- **The function test** on every novum — especially the four machine nova: what the plot turns on is *understood*, not merely believed.
- **Cold-reader pass** — not braced into the procedural-of-loss monotone by the midpoint; the major key met before it.
- **Dyad named per story in the changelog** — equation and mirror, one line each.

Book-specific gates: the bright-sky spine legible across year-stamps; QUORUM among the first three; ≥1 first-person POV; ≤2 adjacent document-built stories; the anthropomorphism ban on the four machine stories.
